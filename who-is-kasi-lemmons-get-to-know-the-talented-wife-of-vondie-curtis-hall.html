Biography of an actress turned director

Kasi’s parents divorced when she was just eight, and the woman moved with her mum to Newton, Massachusetts. The director attended the Commonwealth School in the Back Bay neighbourhood of Boston, Massachusetts. She knew that she had an artistic side to her and felt that she had to become an actress to express herself.
