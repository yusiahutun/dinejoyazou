Prince William fans gush over the young royal who was a dreamy 'Disney Prince Charming'

Prince William’s female fan following in his younger days mirrored that of a rock star. The Prince of Wales has taken a more serious role as a senior royal for years now. However, there was a time when he was the family’s most charming member who dazzled fans in every public appearance.

Long before he became the Duke of Cambridge and the Prince of Wales, Prince William was the royal family’s rockstar. Years before the prince began sneaking Kate off on secret dates, he had a massive female fan following. This Prince William ‘mania’ was perfectly depicted in Netflix’s The Crown’s final episodes. The episode Willsmania shows just how much the ladies loved Princess Diana‘s firstborn in his teenage years and his 20s. A video on TikTok also captures Willsmania of the 90s and 2000s. Its comments section is filled with the ladies still showing a lot of love to the future King of England.

Viral video shows Prince William’s popularity in his younger days

A video shared by the page, Lovely Catherine, shows a teen William greeting a sea of excited women as they hand him a bouquet and more gifts. It also focuses on the Prince of Wales’ stunningly sharp looks as he greatly resembles the late Princess Diana.

At one point he is also seen holding a baby in his hands during a church service. The Prince’s popularity grew meteorically in the wake of his mother’s death in 1997. The ‘Willsmania’ continued until his marriage to Kate Middleton in 2010.

Not only was he was heartthrob on his home turf in the UK but across the world in places like Canada, Australia, New Zealand, and more places.

Fans think William resembled a Disney Prince

Under the viral TikTok, several fans have commented sharing their thoughts on the younger Prince William.

One fan called his younger version a “Disney Prince.” A second fan expressed how in her younger days William was her “fairytale dream guy.”

A third fan also called the royal “Princess Diana’s twin.”

A fourth fan said, “He still looks super handsome today. Can’t wait to see what their (Kate and William) children will look like in the future.”

William’s romances before Kate came along

His dashing looks, royal status, charming personality, and massive fan following meant William had no shortage of beautiful girls who wanted to date him.

The Prince reportedly dated a wealthy girl named Jecca Craig in 1998. They broke up in 1999 but remained friends. He even traveled to her family’s wildlife conservancy near Mount Kenya in 2001. The two went separate ways before William entered St Andrew’s where met his future wife Kate.

The Daily Mail notes, that the Prince laid his eyes on Davina Duckworth-Chad in the summer of 1999 when he boarded a yacht filled with beauties and sailed to the Aegean Sea. Davina’s family was closely connected to the crown.

Not much is known about the Prince’s relationship with Rose Farquhar but it is said that she was his “first love.”

However, the Prince found both his wife and his future queen in none other than his St Andrews classmate Kate.
