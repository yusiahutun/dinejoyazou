20 phone addiction quotes and messages to share on social media

In the digital world where screens outshine reality, the battle against phone addiction is a tough one. However, phone addiction quotes can help you jolt out of the digital trance and start a healthier relationship with your phone. They are perfect reminders to reclaim your presence, foster genuine connections and rediscover life’s beauty beyond the screen.

Phone addiction is an exaggerated and compulsive reliance on smartphones, disrupting daily functioning, interpersonal relationships, and overall well-being. It is characterised by an incapacity to control or restrict usage and a sense of distress when physically separated from the phone.
