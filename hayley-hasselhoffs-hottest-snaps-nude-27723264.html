Hayley made history when she became the first plus-size model to appear on the cover of Playboy Magazine in Germany.

Speaking about the cover shoot, she told The Times: "I thought: I’m 28 years old. I have my own rights over my body and I know how I like to celebrate it.

"Size inclusivity is so important for women who may look at Playboy as a magazine that represents an idealistic beauty standard.
