Love Island's Molly axed in 'biggest twist ever' as Kady arrives and steals her man

Love Island star Molly Marsh has been dumped from the villa in the show's "biggest twist ever".

The 21-year-old star was forced to leave the villa during Friday's (June 24) dramatic episode after losing her man Zachariah Noble to new arrival Kady McDermott.

In one of the show's most brutal ever eliminations, Kady was forced couple up with one of the boys in the villa.

She eventually decided to pick Zach but, in a Love Island twist, her decision meant that his partner Molly had to be sent home and leave the villa with immediate effect.

ITV2 viewers were left gobsmacked at the bombshell news and rushed to share their thoughts on social media.

Taking to Twitter, one fan penned: "Damn! Molly I am gutted giiirl! #LoveIsland"

Another added: "Im actually gutted for #molly strongest couple gone #LoveIsland #LoveIslandUK good god."

A third person said: "I can’t believe am very sad for Molly that was very brutal dump #LoveIsland."

While the girls were away, former series two Islander Kady made her dramatic return to the villa.

Leaving the boys almost speechless, Kady interrupted their boys’ day, announcing: "Alright boys, who's ready for a pocket rocket?"

She wasted no time in gathering the boys around the fire pit and asking who is completely closed off, telling the boys to be true to themselves and be honest.

As Sammy Root and Mehdi Edno took Kady for a villa tour, she jokingly called down from the terrace to the others: "Looking for my Romeo!"

Later on, Tyrique Hyde suggested taking a selfie with Kady and the boys to send to the girls while they were still at the spa.

Throughout the day Kady explored her options, pulling the boys for chats and getting to know all of her fellow Islanders.
