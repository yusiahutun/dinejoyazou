Who Is Lisa Vidal Husband? Married Life, Family &amp; Cancer

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: February 07, 2020

Actress and producer Lisa Vidal- who initially started acting at the age of fourteen in Repertory Theatre- is best known for her roles in TV series like Third Watch (1999-2001), The Division (2001-2004), ER (2001-2004), Boston Legal (2006), CSI Miami (2009), and Southland (2009-2010).

The actress made her film debut in a small role of a character called Tina with a feature dance film, Delivery Boys, in 1985. Her other movies include Christmas Eve (1986), Mighty Aphrodite (1995), The Third Twin (1997), and Star Trek (2009). 

Lisa Vidal's Married Life With Husband 

The actress is married to Jay Cohen, a real estate agent.

The couple tied the knot on 6th January 1990; they have been together for more than thirty years.

Battle With Breast Cancer

The Being Mary Jane (2013) actress was diagnosed with breast cancer in January 2016; she opened up about her disease in an episode of The Real in November 2016. 

Lisa stated that she had invasive ductal carcinoma, which is said to be the most common type of cancer, according to the Mayo Clinic. And she further added,

The reason I really wanted to talk about this, not only to share the experience, but to also say that early diagnosis is so crucial and mammograms don’t always find it.

As of now, the actress is healthy and is cured of cancer.

Age & Family Details

Lisa was born on 13th June 1965 in New York City. Her parents are Manny Vidal and Josie Vidal. Her father worked as a tax consultant and businessman while her mother was a secretary. The actress has two sisters: Christina and Tanya, and a brother named Christian.
