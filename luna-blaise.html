Luna Blaise Biography, Age, Height, Boyfriend, Net Worth

Luna Blaise is an American actress, model, singer, and songwriter. She is extremely known for her role in the TV series ‘Fresh Off The Boat’. Luna also garnered attention after her role in NBC’s ‘Manifest’ and she became popular worldwide. She also won the ‘Young Artist Award’ for her incredible performance as Nicole.

Early Life

Luna Blaise Boyd was born on October 1, 2001, in Los Angeles, California. She is the daughter of Angelyna Martinez, the actress, and Paul Boyd, a famous director. Blaise belongs to mixed ethnicity because her mother being a Mexican American from Texas and her father from Scotland. Her father is a film director and her mother a talent manager. Because of them both being in the industry, Blaise took to the industry like a duck to the water. She has a brother, River Boyd. Her educational background hasn’t been disclosed yet.

Luna Blaise Career

Blaise was very good at acting and performing from a small age. Her parents were amazed when they discovered that she could recite complex dialogues easily. At the age of 5 itself, her parents began taking her to commercial auditions. And she soon began appearing in nationwide ads for reputed brands like KFC, Target, McDonald’s, Gap. Along with these acts, she also did some child modeling work. In 2009, she debuted with a cameo appearance in the film ‘Vicious Circle’. In 2013, she appeared as Young Nina in the film ‘Memoria’ along with James Franco. After that, she was the lead in the music video ‘Sweatshirt’ by Jacob Sartorius appearing as his love interest.

After this, she got the role of Olive Stone in the tv series ‘Manifest’ in 2018. Along with being an actor, she also released songs of her own through her YouTube channel.
