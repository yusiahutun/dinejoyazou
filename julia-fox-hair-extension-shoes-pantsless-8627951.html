See Julia Fox's Shoes Made Out of Hair Extensions

Julia Fox is taking her quirky sense of style to new hair-raising levels — quite literally. 

On Thursday, the actress stepped out to promote her new E! show OMG Fashun in New York City while wearing an outfit that only she could pull off. Taking the no-pants trend for a spin, Fox wore an oversized black blazer with matching briefs by Sho Konishi that were embellished with grooming tools – including nail clippers, cuticle cutters, scissors, and metal razors strategically sewn directly onto the crotch.

But somehow that wasn't even the most bizarre part of her look. 

On her feet, Fox slipped on a pair of black mule stilettos that were covered in long platinum blonde hair extensions that looked like a trip hazard just waiting to happen. The hair on her feet perfectly coordinated with the wig atop her head, which was pinned on the sides with silver clips and included a set of hair rollers. 

Fox finished off her over-the-top outfit with a white top with a lacy neckline, sheer black stockings, and a white handbag. Meanwhile, her glam included bleached brows, smoky blue eyeshadow, and a generous swipe of pink lip gloss. 

Just days earlier, the Uncut Gems actress repped the pantsless look yet again — but this, paired with a completely different hairstyle. Heading to the Knicks vs. vs. Oklahoma City Thunder game at Madison Square Garden on Sunday, Fox wore white underwear with a zipper down the front with a matching top that featured cone-shaped breasts and a new golden dye job that looked like Velveeta mac n' cheese.
