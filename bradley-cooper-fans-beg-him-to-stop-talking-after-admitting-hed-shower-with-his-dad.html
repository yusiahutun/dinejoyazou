Bradley Cooper fans beg him to 'stop talking' after admitting he'd shower with his dad

Bradley Cooper reduced his fan base to begging after he made a string of startling revelations including taking showers with his dad. 

Though celebrities are often placed on a pedestal above us humble mortals, they have a way of reminding us of their humanity. This is one of those moments and one that forced his fanbase to make a desperate plea for Bradley Cooper‘s silence. 

Bradley Cooper said he used to ‘take showers with his dad’

The 49-year-old Hollywood actor appeared in the most recent episode of Dax Shepherd’s Armchair Expert podcast where he released a series of questionable secrets.

Firstly, Dax Shepherd admitted to being “naked all the time” with his family, to which Bradley confirmed this was the case in his own childhood home.

“And by the way, I was like that,” the Maestro star continued.

“Not with my mom but with my dad. My dad was always nude, always took showers with him.”

When Dax further explored the topic and asked if he was comfortable being naked, Bradley simply confirmed “totally.”

It took a while for the star to connect with his daughter

Another strange moment came when Bradley admitted to feeling disconnected from his daughter at first.

“The first eight months — I don’t even know if I really love the kid,’ he said. “It’s dope. It’s cool. I’m watching this thing morph.”

“That’s my experience. Fascinated by it. Loved taking care of it. But would I die if someone came in with a gun?” he added.

Bradley did admit things began to change around eight months later when he suddenly understood why parents would put themselves in danger for their kids. 

Bradley Cooper fans have just one request

Though fans love the Star is Born Again frontman, they have asked him to stop revealing private details about his life.

On Twitter, one person wrote: “What happened to keeping things private?”

“How does this come up in an interview? Like.. what was asked?” another said, while a third added, “Everyday it’s something new with this man.”
