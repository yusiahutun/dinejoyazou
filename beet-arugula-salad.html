How to Make Beet Arugula Salad

For extra-zesty flavor, toss beets with our citrusy dressing up to 2 hours ahead and chill until serving.

Serving Size:

6

Active Time:

15 min.

Total Time:

15 min.

Ingredients

  • 1 lime
  • ½ cup light oil & vinegar dressing
  • 1 (14.5 oz.) can sliced beets, drained
  • 1 (5 oz.) cont. baby arugula
  • ½ cup crumbled blue cheese or herbed goat cheese

Instructions

  • Grate ½ tsp. zest and squeeze 1½ Tbs. juice from lime into large bowl; stir in salad dressing until blended. Add sliced beets to bowl and toss until coated.
  • Arrange arugula on plates; top with beets and dressing remaining in bowl. Sprinkle with cheese. Garnish as desired.
