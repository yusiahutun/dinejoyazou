Perro de Presa Mallorquin is a powerful and agile breed.

The Perro de Presa Mallorquin, also known as the Mallorquin Mastiff, is a large and muscular dog breed that is renowned for its strength and agility. This breed has a rich history in the Balearic Islands.

Originating from the Balearic Islands of Spain, the Perro de Presa Mallorquin has a long and storied history. It was initially developed as a working and guarding breed, tasked with protecting livestock and properties. Throughout the years, it has gained recognition for its loyalty, bravery, and protective nature.
