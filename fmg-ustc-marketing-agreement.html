FMG &amp; USTC Marketing Agreement

CHESTER COUNTY, PA (CelebrityAccess MediaWire) — Focus Management Group (FMG) and officials at United Sports Training Center (USTC) today announced a marketing and promotion agreement for the suburban Philadelphia facility. The multi-year agreement, effective October 12, 2007, will encompass marketing, promotion, advertising and naming rights sponsorship for the complex.

The USTC, located in Downingtown, PA, opened in 1995. The 100 acre complex is located in the affluent Chester County suburb of Philadelphia. The complex consists of two major indoor facilities covering 127,000 square feet offering four (4) regulation basketball courts, eight (8) volleyball courts and three (3) deck hockey fields. The indoor facilities host inline hockey, soccer, lacrosse, field hockey, basketball, football and volleyball. The complex hosts numerous regional trade shows and conventions – including a recent visit and speech by President George W. Bush. The complex also includes eleven (11) outdoor soccer fields, 7,000 square feet of meeting/conference space, two (2) restaurants, a rehabilitation center and a 4,000 square foot health/athletic facility. The complex is privately owned and hosts over 1.2 million guests annually. –by CelebrityAccess Staff Writers
