You can now buy Amal Clooney's royal wedding dress Buy Amal Clooney's yellow Stella McCartney dr

WPA Pool//Getty Images

Amal Clooney looked sensational when she turned up to watch the nuptials of Prince Harry and Meghan Markle this May wearing a yellow Stella McCartney dress. And, although the frock was custom-made for Clooney, the British designer has now released the dress for sale, meaning you can channel her exact look.

The canary yellow dress is perfect for weddings but could easily be taken into your workwear wardrobe too. The crepe design features a pleated neckline and tie around the middle, which flows down below the hem of the midi dress.

For the royal wedding, Clooney teamed the dress with a matching yellow hat, pointed gold court shoes and a metallic clutch bag.

The dress was one of the most-talk-about ensembles of the big day, with global fashion search engine Lyst seeing a huge spike in people searching for dresses that looked like Clooney's. To be more specific, 17,000 people searched for a 'yellow Stella McCartney dress' in just two days following the event, while searches for 'yellow dresses' were up 1,500 per cent week on week.

And now you can buy Clooney's exact design, and also the Stella McCartney dress that Meghan wore for her evening reception. Although it has sold out in the white hue that the duchess wore, you can still snap one up in navy.
