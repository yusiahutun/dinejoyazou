Adria Arjona

Adria Arjona Torres is a Guatemalan and Puerto Rican actress best known for her roles in the HBO television series “True Detective” and the CBS television drama “Person of Interest.

What is the Net Worth of Adria Arjona? Salary, Earnings

According to reliable sources, Arjona’s net worth is estimated to be around $1 million as of late 2022, accumulated over her years as an actor in both film and television since 2012.

Where was Adria Arjona born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Adria was born in Puerto Rico to Leslie Torres, a Puerto Rican mother, and Ricardo Arjona, a Guatemalan father. Her father is a popular Latin American singer-songwriter. Arjona grew up in Mexico City, but her father, who took her on tours, exposed her to travel. She was exposed to various types of art, music, and the bohemian lifestyle at a young age as a result of her frequent travel.

Unfortunately, not much else is known about her childhood and adolescence. Arjona moved to the United States with her family when she was 12, settling in Miami, but at the age of 19, she decided to move to New York City on her own to pursue her dream of becoming an actress. While studying acting at the Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute, she worked as a waitress in several New York restaurants.

Is Adria Arjona Single? Relationship

In terms of her personal life, Arjona is still unmarried, and there have been no reports of her dating anyone. She prefers to keep her personal life private and avoids making the personal information public.

How tall is Adria Arjona? Weight, Hair Color

According to her body measurements, Arjona is 5ft 7ins (1.74 m) tall and weighs approximately 125 lbs (57 kg). She is 35-24-35 inches tall and has brown hair and brown eyes.

How did Adria Arjona start her Professional Career?

Career She landed a minor role in the 2012 short film “Loss,” and she made her television debut in 2014 as Suzy Houchen in an episode of “Unforgettable.” Her early years as an actor helped to shape both her career and her net worth.

Arjona’s career took off in 2015, when she debuted as Dani Silva in the series “Person of Interest,” followed by Helena in the hit Netflix series “Narcos” the following year. She got her big break in 2015 when she was cast as Emily in six episodes of HBO’s “True Detective,” alongside Collin Farrell. The success of the series, as well as her other endeavors, aided her career and increased her wealth.

Arjona is still working as an actor, having appeared in films like “The Belko Experiment,” “Pacific Rim Uprising,” and “Life of the Party.” She also portrayed Dorothy Gale in “Emerald City,” and she will play Anathema Device in “Good Omens.”
