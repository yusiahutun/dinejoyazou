What is the Age of Nastia Liukin?

Nastia Liukin was born on 30th October 1989, in Moscow, Russia SFSR, Soviet Union. As of 2021, she is at the age of 32 years while her horoscope is Scorpio. Her full name is Anastasia Valeryevna Lyukina. She is the only child of Anna Kotchneva (mother) and Valeri Liukin (father). Her father, Valeri is a Russian-American gymnast coach while her mother, Anna is a former Soviet rhythmic gymnast.

Her whole family is a member of the Russian Orthodox church. When Nastia was just 2.5 years old, the family moved to the United States as an immigrates. Nastia holds dual nationality, Russian-American while she belongs to mixed ethnical background.

Did Nastia Liukin attend college?

Yes, Nastia attended several colleges to complete her studies. At first, she joined the Spring Creek Academy in Plano, Texas, and graduated from there in the year 2007. After that, she joined the Southern Methodist University in Dallas, Texas, and studied international business there. Also, she studied sports management and psychology at New York University, in New York City, New York.
