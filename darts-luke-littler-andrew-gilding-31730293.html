Darts fans rage as shocked Andrew Gilding is booed before Luke Littler Ally Pally clash

Darts fans were furious when spectators at Ally Pally were heard Andrew Gilding.

The former world champion had the unenviable task of taking on 16-year-old darts sensation Luke Littler in the second round of the Paddy Power PDC World Darts Championship. Twenty four hours before, Littler made a huge statement by whitewashing Christian Kist 3-0, finishing the clash with a terrifying average of 106.12.

Gilding, 53, was up next for the youngster and while he made a good fist of the challenge, Littler proved to be too good for him. While many darts fans are backing Littler to go on and win the competition, viewers at home were furious when the popular Gilding was jeered by spectators.
