Khlo Kardashian Wore a Tiny Gucci Bikini With a Giant Faux-Fur Coat in the Snow

Posing against a snowy backdrop in a bikini has become a Kardashian-Jenner rite of passage of sorts over the years. Kourtney's done it in a cowboy hat, while Kendall staved off hypothermia in a pair of Miu Miu moon boots. And Kim, who started it all back in 2015, wore what she dubbed a "furkini" for a casual stroll through the snow.  

Now, it's Khloé's turn, and her take on the family tradition is just as sexy. Wearing a tiny Gucci bikini covered in the brand's interlocking GG logo underneath a giant faux-fur coat that was slightly draped off her shoulders, the Good American founder accessorized with a coordinating black floppy hat and snow boots. Like any other photoshoot, Khloé had on full glam, styling her blonde hair in long, face-framing layers and complementing her bronzy glow with a frosty nude lip. 

"It’s giving mob wife vibes…. Faux mob wife of course,” she captioned the snapshots on Instagram, assuring fans that both her fur hat and coat were not real.

Khloé is currently in Aspen, Colorado with her sisters Kendall and Kim, who have also shared their fair share of vacation content. Last week, Kim documented their sibling time on the slopes, sporting a black leather Chanel ski suit first worn by Victoria Beckham in 1996, and Kendall, for her part, kept warm inside while dressed in a long black gown with a barely-there back and a plunging neckline that scooped past her bellybutton.
