Carolyn Manno’s Bio, Wiki

Carolyn Manno was born on July 16, 1984, in Florida, USA. Her actual birth date is not revealed. She grew up as a tomboy and has a keen interest in games like football and soccer from an early age.

Regarding her education, she attended the University for Florida and graduated with a major in telecommunications.

Right after her graduation, she started her career as a news reporter in South Bend. After working three years in South Bend, she reported news on The Super Bowl, Stanley Cup, as well as on The NBA finals. After her interest aroused on reporting various sports events, she began continuing her career covering and reporting the sports news.

Moreover, after serving various channels she got a huge breakthrough. She got a chance to report the news for Clubs White Sox, Notre Dame Fighting Irish, Chicago Bears. Afterward, she moved to Comcast Sports Net. There she covered the main events like The Super Bowl and The NBA Finals. Similarly, in 2009 she was hired by NBC and after four years she became a full-time reporter for NBC and NBCSN.
