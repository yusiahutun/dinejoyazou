Meghan Markle Went Casual to Reunite With Her 'Suits' Co-Stars

Every now and then, Meghan Markle likes to keep things casual and dress down — especially if it's for a good cause.

Markle appeared alongside her Suits co-stars, Kelly McKee Zajfen and Abigail Spencer, in a t-shirt by Alliance of Moms and a pair of well-fitting blue jeans. In photos shared to the organization's official Instagram, all three women were pictured in a fitted white shirt that read "Love Like a Mother" in red '70s-inspired lettering — for Markle's take on the look, she opted for a high-waisted pair of relaxed denim and accessorized with rings and an assortment of brass and gold colored bracelets on her left hand.

Alliance for Moms captioned the carousel post, “When we speak about the people in our lives who lift us up when you need it with undeniable force of pure love, I think of these two. You inspire me. You pick me up when I need it, and you Love Like A Mother! Thank you for continuing to support the Alliance of Moms for supporting this campaign and our youth. In doing so, you are supporting the mission to build bright futures for young parents who have experienced foster care.”

This isn't the first time that Markle has worn a t-shirt for the organization — in May 2023, Zajfen shared a photo of her and her Suits co-star to the Alliance of Moms' Instagram account. The pair wore a fitted white t-shirt, both of which read "Community Motherhood." At the time, Zajfen captioned the post, "As Mother’s Day approaches, I can’t help but think of my own #commUNITY of Motherhood. I instantly think of you, Meg. How incredibly nurturing and warm and open you are. What an incredible friend and mother. I’m truly in awe of you, and I am so grateful you are part of my #CommUNITYofmotherhood."

Markle's latest cameo appearance on Instagram comes amid the forthcoming launch of her lifestyle brand, American Riviera Orchard. The first product from the line, a jam limited to 50 quantities ahead of its launch, was revealed via Instagram through her friend Delfina Blaquier. While a release date for the brand has yet to be revealed, the Duchess is reportedly very excited for the brand's arrival.

"It feels authentic to her. She can’t wait for the website to launch," a source told People. "This is something she’s been wanting to do for a while. She is excited to share her style and things that she loves.”
