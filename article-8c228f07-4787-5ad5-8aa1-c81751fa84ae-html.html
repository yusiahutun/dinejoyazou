Jailed gang members snitch list backfires | Local News

A “snitch list” that was created by a jailed gang member was intercepted by investigators with the Jones County Sheriff’s Department, and now the inmate and a Soso woman are facing more charges.

Christopher Joe “JoJo” Dennis, 34, of Silver Creek, who is in jail for his role in the theft of a safe containing more than $100,000 in cash and thousands of dollars’ worth of guns, jewelry and other items, reportedly prepared the list and intended for it to be shared in private groups on social media, according to a press release from the Jones County Sheriff’s Department.
