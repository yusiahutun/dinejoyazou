Native's Desert-Inspired Fall Escape Body Care Line Just Launched

All products and services featured are independently chosen by editors. However, StyleCaster may receive a commission on orders placed through its retail links, and the retailer may receive certain auditable data for accounting purposes.

I don’t know about you, but when fall time rolls around, I get so sick and tired of smelling the same sickly sweet scents. While pumpkin spice latte, smoky marshmallow, and cinnamon apple all have their place, I think there is more to autumn than gourmand fragrances — and Native does, too.

The MVP of GRWM routines across America has done it yet again, coming out with a limited-edition personal care line that will transport you to the desert without leaving your bathroom. Fall Escape is all about what the name suggests — escaping. Inspired by the natural beauty of the Southwest, Native’s new collection features ingredients straight from the desert. Instead of apples, think cactus blossoms. Trade pumpkin spice for pinon pine, and say bye-bye to the basics ‘ol scent standbys.

Let me introduce you to the four new fragrances available in the clean, hardworking deodorant (regular, sensitive, and plastic-free), body wash, facial cleanser, and hair care formulas we’ve come to know and love. Starting off, we have Honey & Saguaro, which is a warm, citrusy blend with a hint of peach. If the desert were a tonic, it would smell like Native’s Sage & Sweet Citrus scent — zesty ginger and herbaceous sage. For floral fans, Native has Vanilla & Cactus Flower, which smells of desert cactus blossoms mixed with a subtle, sweet vanilla cream. Last but not least is Desert Grass & Sandalwood — the scent of sun-drenched sweetgrass blended with warm pear and sandalwood.
