The TikTok-Viral Anthropologie Primrose Mirrors Are 30% Off

TikTok has become the number one goldmine for discovering the latest beauty hacks, summer fashion finds and, best of all, trendy home decor pieces—including the famous vintage-style Primrose Mirror from Anthropologie. The gold statement piece has racked up quite the cult-following over the years, and its many features on the social platform have only skyrocketed its popularity to new heights. 

If you’ve been eyeing the iconic mirror for your own home for some time, I’ve got some stellar news: All sizes and colors are currently on sale this weekend only. That’s right—the stunning mirror just dropped to prices closer within our budget range; from now through Sunday, June 26, all Primrose products are 30 percent off at the retailer.

The design is unlike any other mirror options we’ve seen on the market, so we’d recommend jumping on this limited-time offer ASAP. Louie Higaki, GMM, AnthroLiving, described the artistry behind its appearance, and we’d say it’s pretty unique. “The Gleaming Primrose Mirror was inspired by French Antique Gilt Mirrors seen throughout Paris. As the years have evolved, so has the mirror with new shapes and finishes,” they said. “The mirror is a style chameleon adding drama to any architectural aesthetic retaining her star of the show status within AnthroLiving.” 

We love the drama it’s guaranteed to bring any room, especially considering the brand also says the piece is meant to “evoke the spirit of what could have been found in a Parisian Maison,” with the idea that it would have lived in a grand foyer or entryway during its “original heyday.” With that said, if you’re dealing with a much smaller space (raises hand), the 3-foot option is perfect for hanging on the wall in your living area, or propping up on your desk or vanity table. But, if you are able to go all-out, the 7-foot option is Anthropologie’s most popular seller and is discounted by a whopping $480.

Additional sizing options are a 5-foot option, and a 6-foot floor option, both of which are also available in the four beautiful shades—black, gold, silver and Verdegris. 

This feature sale only runs through the weekend, so let’s just say there’s no better time to treat yourself than literally right now.
