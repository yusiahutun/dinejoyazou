Olivia Rodrigo was spotted out and about with her gal pal in New York City on Sunday.

The 20-year-old singer put on a casual display in a gray sweater and black leggings as she headed to a pilates class and then lunch at Joseph Leonard restaurant with her best friend, Madison Hu.

She wore tall, white socks and black sneakers as she made her way to her workout class, carrying a woven shoulder bag.

The Vampire hitmaker — who was spotted snapping a selfie with Sara Bareilles over the weekend — tied her brunette tresses into two pigtail braids and sported a pair of cat-eye sunglasses.

For her outing with her Bizaardvark costar, she appeared to be sporting a makeup-free look minus a bright pink lip to highlight her natural beauty.

Out in the city: Olivia Rodrigo, 20, was spotted out and about with her gal pal in New York City on Sunday

The three-time Grammy winner — who received six nominations for her second album GUTS — accessorized simply with various dainty rings.

At one point, the actress was spotted chatting with her close pal, who sported a similar outfit for their outing. 

Hu, who made an appearance in Rodrigo's music video for Bad Idea Right?, also wore black leggings, tall white socks, and dark sneakers for their workout class. 

She bundled up in a royal blue puffer jacket and carried a cream crossbody, drawstring bag.

Earlier this week, it was announced that Rodrigo received a total of six nominations for her latest album and a number of her songs off that tracklist. 

Her hit single, Vampire, received three nominations: Record of the Year, Song of the Year and Best Pop Solo Performance.

Her 12-song album, GUTS, is nominated for Album of the Year and Best Pop Vocal Album.

And her track, Ballad of a Homeschooled Girl, received a nomination for Best Rock Song.

GUTS followed her debut studio album SOUR, which was released in May 2021.

At the 64th annual Grammy Awards, SOUR won Best Pop Vocal Album and Best Pop Solo Performance for her viral hit, Drivers License.

She also won the award for Best New Artist that same year.

The upcoming awards ceremony will be on February 4, 2024. Shortly afterwards, Rodrigo will embark on her GUTS World Tour.
