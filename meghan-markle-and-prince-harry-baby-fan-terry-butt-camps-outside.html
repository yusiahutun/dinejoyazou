Royal Superfan Terry Butt Camps Outside Windsor Castle for Meghan and Harrys Baby for His 84th Bi

What’s one way to show you’re ecstatic about Prince Harry and Meghan Markle‘s baby? You camp outside Windsor Castle like royal superfan Terry Butt is doing.

The Duchess of Sussex, 37, will be giving birth to her first child with the duke, 34, any day now, and Terry is more than ready to welcome the baby — he’s been camping outside the castle in hopes to celebrate the minute the little one arrives! Wearing a United Kingdom outfit from head to toe, including quite the hat, Terry waits outside with a sign with that reads, “I hope it’s today! Meghan & Harry, it’s my 84th birthday today!” Now that’s dedication!

When asked what would be the best birthday gift he could receive, the enthusiastic fan told Express, “I am 84 and the newborn baby, of course, I don’t mess around, you know.” However, Terry does admit that he was a bit let down when he learned the royal duo wanted to keep the birth of their baby private.

“Where people are disappointed, we’re all disappointed, but there is Meghan and Harry’s privacy and that comes first,” he said.

The world continues to wait for the royal baby, but no one has a clue when he or she will arrive, not even Prince William. “I haven’t got my phone on me. I have no idea!” the 36-year-old joked on Saturday, April 27, when asked by a fan if there were any signs of the royal baby. “You guys will find out before I do at this rate!” he added. Although, is there a chance the child’s gender has already been revealed?

Pages on the Kensington palace website (www.royal.uk) have reportedly been reserved for the names “Prince Arthur” (/prince-arthur), “Prince James” (/prince-james) and “Prince Alexander” (/prince-alexander), which may mean that the royal pair will be having a boy, according to Daily Star.

Guess Terry, and the rest of the world, will have to wait and see!
