10 best prayers for Sunday School teachers and students

Prayer is the most critical thing in the life of any believer because it is how you communicate with God. It does not matter whether it is a prayer said privately or in a gathering because it has to be said with humility and respect. Prayers for Sunday school teachers and students are geared towards teaching little children the art of praying.

Young children should be encouraged to pray even as they are taught that the Kingdom of heaven is for those who are childlike. While children are full of humility and purity, they may stray into sin if they are not taught to be prayerful. The role of devotions for Sunday school teachers is to teach a solid spiritual foundation in children so that nothing can sway their beliefs.

Prayers for Sunday school teachers and students

Prayer for Sunday school students should be simple and precise. The students are supposed to learn how to pray on their own based on the prayers taught at Sunday school. The best place to start is a short prayer for students and teachers. Below are the best Sunday school prayers:

Almighty Father, we praise you for those you have called to minister to children in Sunday school. We seek the guidance of the Holy Spirit as we teach the young people to have a thorough knowledge of your word and prepare for salvation. Give us the ability to guide them along the Christian pathway with doctrinally sound teaching. May all Sunday school classes be venues for uplifting the Lord Jesus. In Jesus’ name, Amen!

Opening prayer for Sunday school teachers

A short prayer should always open Sunday school for students and teachers. Such prayers teach about being thankful for the gift of life before praying for other things. They include:

Dear God, we thank you for the gift of life and the gift of our children. We especially put our Sunday school class before you. Bless everyone to know you and dedicate their lives to your service. Let everything we do from the beginning to the end of our class be filled with your love. Bless our ears to listen and understand the stories about you. Let us grow to follow the example of Jesus. Amen.Dear Lord and Father of all, we thank you for today's gift. Thank you for the different ways you provide for all of us. You have shown us your love and protection since we were born, and for that, we will be forever grateful. Please help us focus our hearts and minds as we begin our Sunday school class today. Let your Holy Spirit inspire us to listen attentively. Guide us by your everlasting light as we learn more about your word. We pray in the name of Jesus, Amen!

Closing prayer for Sunday school teachers and students

Like Sunday school begins with opening prayers, it must also end with closing prayers. These can be prayers like:

Heavenly Father, as we end our Sunday school session today and head back to our families, we thank you. We pray that all we learnt will help us to become better people. Help us remember these important lessons each day and show love and affection to others. When we are alone, remind us that Jesus will always be our friend and that we will never be on our own. We thank you in Jesus’ name, Amen. Dear Father in heaven, in Proverbs 22:6, you said, "Teach children how they should live, and they will remember it all their lives." We, therefore, pray that you will bless our Sunday school class to remember what we have taught them today. Let them grow to be God-fearing people and experience the bond of love from their family members; amen.

Prayer for Sunday school teachers' meeting

Sunday school teachers cannot preach water and then drink water. Instead, they have to be prayerful, just like they teach their students. Below are examples of prayers for Sunday school teacher's meetings:

Lord God, we ask you to guide us as we teach young people to be spiritual people. Enlighten us always to follow the example of Jesus. Send your Holy Spirit to guide and protect all children as they pass through our hands. Give us the strength to be good vessels they can emulate in their day to day lives. We know you have good plans for each of them, and we thank you for enabling us to be part of it. Amen.Dear Lord, You sent Jesus to be our greatest teacher as we seek the Kingdom of heaven. Bless us to be humble like Jesus was and follow in all his teachings. Fill us with the virtues of patience and understanding as we teach the little ones. We pray to be shining lights that will guide them to your path. All this we ask you through Christ our saviour, Amen.

Prayer of thanks for Sunday school teachers

Being a Sunday school teacher is a great responsibility that cannot be accomplished without the intervention of God. As a result, these teachers must be prayed for to fulfil their duties in a holly manner with prayers like:

Almighty God, you are the one who gave us our beautiful teachers. We thank you for them and ask you to bless the work of their hands. Thank you for giving them the gift to teach us in a way that we understand. Fill their hearts with courage so that Satan may not take over their lives. Let them have the strength to rise to all challenges they face. Make them kind to continue helping others. In Jesus' name, we pray, Amen.Dear Lord, our Sunday school teachers are our role models. They teach us how to pray well and have a personal relationship with you. Bless their families for supporting them in their call. Let their lives be peaceful and free of all the turmoil caused by evil. Most importantly, let all their students grow to be good people like them. We ask all this in the wonderful name of Jesus, Amen.

Frequently asked questions

  • How do you pray in Sunday School? By bowing your head, closing your eyes and praying to God for what you want.
  • What do you pray for a teacher? All the good things like good health, wisdom and abundant blessings.
  • How do you start a prayer? First, by addressing God because he is the one we are praying to.
  • How do I start a Sunday school lesson? Before opening the bible to teach students, you must start with a word of prayer.
  • What is Sunday school for adults? Even though Sunday school typically provides catechesis for children, the same can apply to adults in need of similar lessons.
  • Can children say the opening prayer in Sunday school? Anyone can say the opening prayer as long as they have genuine intentions.
  • What is the aim of Sunday school? To make children understand the meaning of bible stories and teach them how to pray.
  • Must I kneel to pray? You can kneel, stand, bow, sit or walk while praying because what matters is the condition of the soul and not the position of the body.
