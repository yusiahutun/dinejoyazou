Alessia Cara, Halsey, and Marshmello ft. Anne-Marie Announced As First Performers For IHEARTRADIO MM

TORONTO, ON (CelebrityAccess) – The iHeartRadio MMVAS has today (June 28) announced its first round of performers for the 2018 edition of the show.

Canada’s iconic music awards show welcomes back to the stage 2017 MMVA co-host and hometown girl, Alessia Cara, and features first-time MMVA performances from Halsey and Marshmello ft. Anne-Marie.

Scheduled to take place Sunday, August 26 at 9 p.m. ET, the show will be simulcast on CTV, MTV, and Much.

Additionally, the full, star-studded event will be livestreamed on CTV.ca, CTV GO, Much.com and via Twitter. For the second consecutive year, fans can also livestream the event via the iHeartRadio app, iHeartRadio.ca, and across nine Virgin Radio stations in Toronto, Kitchener, London, Halifax, Montréal, Winnipeg, Edmonton, Calgary, and Vancouver.

Additional artists will be announced in the coming months.
