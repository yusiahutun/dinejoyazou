Katie Holmes is effortlessly chic in a Miu Miu polo shirt and jeans while attending the 2023 US Open

Katie Holmes attended the 2023 US Open Tennis Championships, which were held in New York City on Friday afternoon.

The 44-year-old performer cut a fashionable figure during the sporting event, during which she spent time with one of her friends.

The actress — who has become renowned for her eclectic sense of dress — wore a cream-colored and patterned long-sleeve polo shirt while cheering for the athletes involved in the competition.

Holmes tucked her top into a pair of blue jeans and contrasted it with a pair of jet-black leather ballerina flats from Mansur Gavriel.

The Dawson's Creek star eventually removed her top and wrapped it around her shoulders, revealing a bright white sleeveless undershirt.

Holmes' most recent on-screen appearance was in Rare Objects, which was released this past April.

The movie is based on Kathleen Tessaro's novel of the same name, which was originally published in 2016.

The film is centered on a young woman who attempts to rebuild her life by working at an antique store while dealing with her troubled past.

In addition to co-starring in the movie, Holmes co-wrote its script alongside Phaedon A. Papadopoulos, and served as its director and producer.

The project was her third overall directorial effort, and her first two projects, entitled All We Had and Alone Together, were released in 2016 and 2022, respectively.

The feature's cast also included Julia Mayorga, Derek Luke and Alan Cumming, among other performers.

Holmes spoke about working on the feature during an interview with Variety, where she revealed why she chose to take on the project.

She stated: 'I was drawn to the female friendship and this metaphor of "you are more beautiful for having been broken."'

The filmmaker added that 'the screenplay has gone through a lot of different forms, which was great because you learn exactly the story you want to tell. So, it feels very satisfying to have the movie come out.' 

Rare Objects received primarily mixed reviews from critics, and it currently holds a 43% rating on Rotten Tomatoes.
