Desiree Perez Named CEO Of Roc Nation, Jay Brown Named Vice Chairman

Desiree Perez/Instagram.

NEW YORK (CelebrityAccess) – Desiree Perez has been named CEO of Jay Z’s Roc Nation the company announced today (Dec. 13).

Perez, who previously served as Roc Nation’s COO for a decade, will now be responsible for leading and developing the company’s numerous verticals, which include its record label, management, publishing, film and TV, touring, philanthropy and branding, among others.

Jay Brown, who previously served as its CEO, has been named vice chairman. In his new role, he will continue to cultivate partnerships and expand the Roc Nation brand.

Roc Nation was established in 2008.
