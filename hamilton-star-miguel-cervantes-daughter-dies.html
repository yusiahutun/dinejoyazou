Hamilton star Miguel Cervantes left heartbroken after daughter dies aged three

Adelaide had been battling epilepsy and Infantile Spasms since she was a baby

    star Miguel Cervantes and his family are mourning the loss of their daughter Adelaide, who passed away aged three on Saturday.
