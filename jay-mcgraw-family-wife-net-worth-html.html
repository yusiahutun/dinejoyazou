Who Is Jay McGraw Wife? Married Status, Family &amp; Net Worth Details

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: February 24, 2020

Jay McGraw is an American TV personality, who is best known for hosting the Fox home makeover show, Renovate My Family. Jay has also stepped up as an executive producer of the medical talk show, The Doctors. 

Further, he is also an author, who has penned many self-help books like Life Strategies for Teens, Closing The Gap: A Strategy for Bringing Parents and Teens Together, and The Ultimate Weight Solution for Teens. 

Jay McGraw Married To Wife

Jay McGraw tied the knot to Erica Dahm on 12th August 2006.

Their wedding ceremony was held at Jay's parents' house in Beverly Hills. 

Jay McGraw with his wife Erica Dahm in December 2019 (Photo: Jay McGraw's Instagram)

The wedding ceremony was very intimate and elegant, which was attended by seventy close friends and relatives. Following the ceremony, a glittering reception was held for 400 guests at the Beverly Hills Hotel. 

Jay McGraw's Wife

Jay's wife, Erica, is a model, best known as one-third of the Dahm triplets with her sisters, Nicole and Jaclyn. Together, the Dahm triplets are known for their appearances in Playboy magazine.
