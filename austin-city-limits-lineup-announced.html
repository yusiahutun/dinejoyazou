Austin City Limits Lineup Announced

AUSTIN, Texas (CelebrityAccess) Paul McCartney, Metallica, Childish Gambino, Arctic Monkeys, Travis Scott and Odesza have been announced as the headliners for this year’s Austin City Limits Music Festival.

The lineup also includes The National, Khalid, Shawn Mendes, Camila Cabello, Hozier, David Byrne, Chvrches, Justice, St. Vincent, Brandi Carlile, and Janelle Monáe.

The 17th annual event takes place over two consecutive weekends in Austin, Oct. 5-7 and Oct. 12-14 at Zilker Park.

Other artists announced: ), Deftones (Weekend One), BØRNS, Manchester Orchestra, Lily Allen (Weekend Two), The Breeders, Houndmouth, Sharon Van Etten (Weekend One), Marian Hill (Weekend Two), Elle King, Bishop Briggs, Bahamas, Mt. Joy, Cuco, and Durand Jones & The Indications.
