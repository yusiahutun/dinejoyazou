7

Caterpillar and Cheshire Cat Costumes

Brit + Co

Looking for a clever way to make use of that old blue Snuggie? This caterpillar costume is for you! Just add a felt strip down the center to define the belly and glue on a bunch of hot pink hand shapes. To make it a group Halloween costume, recruit a friend to dress up as the Cheshire Cat (see Halloween cat makeup ideas). Just dress in head-to-toe pink and add stripes with purple duct tape. A feather boa tail finishes the look.

Get the tutorial at Brit + Co.
