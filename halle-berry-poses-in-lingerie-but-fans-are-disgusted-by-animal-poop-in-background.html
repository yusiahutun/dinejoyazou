Halle Berry poses in lingerie but fans are 'disgusted' by 'animal poop' in background

Actress Halle Berry has posted a picture in which she is sat in between several huge rocks … with poop at her feet.

The Hollywood actress posted the sunny snap yesterday (December 18) and though she is losing seductively and shaded by the huge rock in front of her, there is no hiding what lies around her on the ground. Fans quickly picked up on it and took to the comments to notify the celebrity she might want to watch her step.

Halle Berry posts a picture with poop at her feet for some reason

In her latest Instagram post, Halle can be seen in a sunny location sitting on a huge rock while wearing a black swimsuit. However, she’s not the easiest to see as the snap she is shadowed by another rock lodged in front of her.

The movie star captioned the picture: “Couldn’t hide these rocks” and added a winking face emoji.

However, on the floor by the actress’ feet were some pretty dark and questionable shapes that look suspiciously like poop, and fans couldn’t help but feel a little squeezy at the sight. They asked the 57-year-old why she hadn’t left it out of frame, with one commenter calling the decision “disgusting.”
