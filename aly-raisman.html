American gymnast and two-time Olympian, Aly Raisman is the daughter of Lynn (née Faber), a former high school gymnast and Rick Raisman. She graduated from Needham High School in 2012. She completing her senior year via online classes while training for the Olympics. Raisman is a member and captain of both the 2012 "Fierce Five" and 2016 "Final Five" U.S. women's Olympic gymnastics, which won their respective team competitions.

In July, Aly Raisman was the focus of a documentary on Comcast Sports Net titled 'Aly Raisman: Quest for Gold'. She is a contestant on season 16 of Dancing With the Stars and finished in fourth place with two-time champion Mark Ballas. In 2016, Olympics in Rio de Janeiro, Raisman won a gold medal in the team event, making her and teammate Gabby Douglas the only Americans with back-to-back team golds.
