Logic kicks out fan from his Austin concert after audience member whistled

Rapper Logic removed a fan from his Austin concert after the audience member 'f**ked up' his show by whistling. 

Videos of the moment have been posted to social media, showing the 33-year-old calling out the audience member who continued making the noise despite Logic's objections. 

'I can hear you whistling, shut the f**k up,' Logic, who is expecting his second child with wife Brittney Noell, first jokingly admonished the problematic audience member. When the whistling persisted, Logic said, 'I'm going to kick you the f**k out of here if you do not shut up. Think I'm joking? Think I'm joking?' 

Finally, Logic had had enough. 'Okay, who is it? Who whistling like a b**ch?' he asked. 

'Where? Where they at?... F**k you, you f**king up my show,' he said. 'I'm sitting here, trying to talk about a story of peace, love and positivity, and everybody so they can f**king follow their own dreams and do what they want, and you being a b***h.

'Who was it? I'll wait,' Logic - born Sir Robert Bryson Hall II - said before scanning the crowd to locate the audience member.

'Was it her? Then get the f**k on out of here. I asked you not to do something and you f**king did it. F**k all that s**t. I'll wait. 

'Y'all want to have a good time tonight? You goddamn right. What do I respect? What do I talk about?

'I talk about respect, unity, love, equality for every man, woman, and child, regardless of race, religion, color, creed, and sexual orientation. I'm sitting here trying to spread a positive message and somebody wanna be a b**ch. I see him, I see where he is. I see him, he's right there.

'Y'all gonna get the f**k on out of here. Where they at? There he goes. They're right there. Yup, get the f**k on out of my show.

'You said what, you said f**k me? I can't hear what you say. What I do know is, you can be excited, you can whistle as loud as you want, but if I ask you politely to let me speak to my amazing fans, I hope that you do... you said it was "for you", is that what you said?

'That's too bad, I asked you to be quiet and then you didn't, you didn't, you didn't, and you didn't. But that's okay, I'm not going to argue with stupid people. Let's keep it going.'

Hitting the road: The 33-year-old is currently on The College Park Tour 

Logic, who has two Grammy nominations under his belt, embarked on The College Park Tour back in May, and has stops scheduled for Los Angeles, San Diego, and Phoenix, Arizona soon. 

Logic released his eighth studio album, College Park, in February, with the likes of RZA, Norah Jones, and Seth MacFarlane making appearances.

The rapper announced he and wife Brittney Noell are expecting their second child together back in January.

The couple, who married in 2019, also share nearly three-year-old son Bobby.

Logic was previously married to singer Jessica Andrea for three years.
