Drayton Manor Zoo is home to over 100 species of animals.

Drayton Manor Zoo is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts, boasting a diverse collection of over 100 different animal species. From lions and tigers to monkeys and giraffes, visitors can witness a wide variety of fascinating creatures in their natural habitats.

It is the only zoo in the UK with four endangered Amur tigers.

Drayton Manor Zoo takes pride in its conservation efforts and is home to four endangered Amur tigers. These magnificent creatures are under threat in the wild, and their presence in the zoo helps raise awareness about the importance of protecting their species.

The zoo offers a unique animal encounters experience.

Visitors at Drayton Manor Zoo have the opportunity to get up close and personal with some of the animals through the zoo’s animal encounters experience. From feeding giraffes to handling reptiles, this unforgettable experience allows guests to learn more about the animals and their conservation needs.

Drayton Manor Zoo is actively involved in several breeding programs.

The zoo plays a vital role in conservation by participating in various breeding programs, including those for endangered species such as the Sumatran tiger and the black lemur. These programs aim to increase the population of these rare animals and protect them from extinction.

It offers educational programs for children.

Drayton Manor Zoo is dedicated to educating the younger generation about wildlife and conservation. The zoo offers interactive educational programs, including workshops and guided tours, to enhance children’s understanding of the animal kingdom and the importance of environmental preservation.

The zoo is set within the beautiful grounds of Drayton Manor Park.

Drayton Manor Zoo is situated within the stunning surroundings of Drayton Manor Park, a vast 280-acre estate in Staffordshire, England. Visitors can enjoy the picturesque scenery while exploring the zoo and experiencing thrilling rides and attractions in the park.

There are daily animal shows and demonstrations.

At Drayton Manor Zoo, visitors can witness exciting animal shows and demonstrations that showcase the natural behaviors and abilities of various species. From falconry displays to sea lion presentations, these shows offer entertainment and educational opportunities for visitors of all ages.

The zoo takes part in important conservation research.

Drayton Manor Zoo actively engages in conservation research to contribute to the understanding and protection of wildlife. The zoo collaborates with universities and scientific organizations to conduct studies on animal behavior, habitat preservation, and other important aspects of conservation.

It provides state-of-the-art animal welfare facilities.

The wellbeing of the animals is of utmost importance at Drayton Manor Zoo. The zoo ensures that its facilities provide a comfortable and safe environment for the animals, with spacious enclosures that mimic their natural habitats and access to proper veterinary care.
