20 Best Linen Dresses, Tested &amp; Reviewed by a Fashion Editor 2024

Best Linen Shirt Dress

Jenni Kayne Day Dress

Best Linen Shirt Dress

Jenni Kayne Day Dress

Credit: Jenni Kayne

Jenni Kayne's linen Day dress is my absolute ideal on the hottest summer days. It's one of those pieces that's minimal to the untrained eye and incredibly considered for discerning shoppers. I found that the pleating along the back and the smaller buttons made this dress feel much more elegant than other styles I've tried. The colors are light, so they won't absorb too much heat; the short-sleeve cut means I can wear a regular bra underneath. I've followed the brand's styling suggestion here and worn it with a belt cinched at the waist—but I can assure that compliments are guaranteed when it's worn without accessories as well. I haven't worn this dress on a trip, but its refined-yet-relaxed attitude makes me feel like I'm vacationing in Nantucket when I wear it.

Sizes: XS–XXL

Colors: White, Khaki

What reviewers are saying: "I took a chance on this dress recently and I’m certainly glad I did. The linen feels lovely and the khaki color does not disappoint. The cut is incredibly flattering—nipped at the waist but not too tight, shoulder seams dropped ever so slightly. It can be casual and simple, but it’s also easy to style if you want to add your own touch."

Best Structured Linen Dress

Farm Rio Side Laced Linen Blend Dress

Best Embroidered Linen Dress

Rixo Benedict Embroidered Linen-Blend Midi Dress

Best Embroidered Linen Dress

Rixo Benedict Embroidered Linen-Blend Midi Dress

Rixo's vintage-inspired dresses get the most play at formal events. Or they used to, before I came across this linen midi dotted with tiny flowers along the bodice and hem. This dress has the throwback appeal I love in Rixo's satin and chiffon pieces, but a cut and breathable fabrication that's travel- and daily-life-friendly. It'll be my Saturday go-to with a lace-up leather sandal and a pair of oversized sunglasses.

Sizes: UK6–UK20

Color: Black Floral

Best Long-Sleeve Linen Dress

Nanushka Gilia Ruched Linen Dress

Best Long-Sleeve Linen Dress

Nanushka Gilia Ruched Linen Dress

Credit: Nanushka

I first got acquainted with Nanushka through its faux leather blazers and coats for fall. The Budapest-based label's linens were a fun discovery I made in time for summer. Best Open-Back Linen Dress
