Lineup Announced For Rolling Loud L.A.

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Organizers for the Rolling Loud festival brand announced the lineup for the upcoming Los Angeles edition of the event, with A$AP Rocky, Chance the Rapper, Future and Lil Uzi Vert leading the bill.

The two-day fest will also feature performances by Meek Mill, Juice WRLD, Young Thug, Lil Baby, YG, Playboi Carti, Tyga, Gunna, DaBaby, $uicideBoy$, Trippie Redd, and Blueface among numerous others.

The festival, which will be the final Rolling Loud event in 2019, lands for its third year in Los Angeles on Dec. 14th & 15th at Banc of California Stadium and Exposition Park.

The festival just wrapped its San Francisco edition this past weekend, drawing more than 40,000 fans with performances by G-Eazy, Lil Uzi Vert, Migos, and Megan Thee Stallion among others.
