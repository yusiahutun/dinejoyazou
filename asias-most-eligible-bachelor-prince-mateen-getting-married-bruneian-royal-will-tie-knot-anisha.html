Asias most eligible bachelor Prince Mateen is getting married! The Bruneian royal will tie the kn

Oh, how we love a royal wedding – even if it means saying goodbye to any fairy tale hopes of landing a bachelor like Prince Abdul Mateen.

The Bruneian royal, who has ranked among the “most eligible bachelors in Asia”, is officially off the market and is set to tie the knot with his fiancée in a dreamy wedding ceremony next January.

So who’s the lucky lady? According to the Independent, the handsome blue blood will marry Anisha Rosnah, the granddaughter of Sultan Hassanal Bolkiah’s special adviser.

The festivities are expected to kick off on January 7 in the capital city of Bandar Seri Begawan. The wedding should span 10 days, including the Islamic ceremony akad nikah to solemnise the marriage on January 11, followed by a reception on January 14, reports Radio Television Brunei. According to British media, the UK’s Prince William and his wife Catherine, Princess of Wales, are likely to be on the exclusive wedding guest list.

Although the Bruneian couple are relatively low-key, they have shown up to high-profile engagements together, including Prince Mateen’s sister Princess Azemah Ni’matul Bolkiah’s wedding, which took place earlier this year.Per Town & Country, Prince Mateen, 32, is Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah of Brunei’s fourth son and 10th child.

The prince has a huge fan base, with 2.4 million followers on Instagram alone, and is sixth in line to the throne, according to the Independent. He is also a trained helicopter pilot and medal-winning polo player, who serves as a major in the Royal Brunei Air Force.
