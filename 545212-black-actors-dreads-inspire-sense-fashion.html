13 black actors with dreads to inspire your sense of fashion

Which famous African Americans have dreadlocks? From Jahi Winston's ethereal elegance to Luka Sabbat's rugged charm, black actors with dreads weave their narratives into every twist and turn of their dreads. Each strand surely tells a story of their creativity, unapologetic self-expression, challenging conventional beauty standards, and celebrating the rich diversity of black hair.

Black stars are known to let their personalities shine through their fashion, from clothing to hairstyles. People enjoy seeing their diversity all year long on tours, red-carpet events, social media, and more.
