Who Is Britta Merwin? Know Her Bio & Wiki

Britta Merwin was born in San Antonia, United States of America. Merwin holds an American nationality and belongs to the white ethnicity. Merwin was born in a military family and spent most of her childhood in Colorado. Since her tender age, Merwin had a dream of being a meteorologist after a local meteorologist visited her school.

Britta studied Meteorology at Stony Brooke University in New York, graduating with a Bachelor of Science in 2006 in Atmospheric Sciences-Meteorology with Cum Laude Honors.

Britta Merwin Profession

Soon after graduation, Britta interned at WCBS-TV, New York and at KMGH, which helped enhance her passion for meteorology. Her professional meteorology career began after working for NBC Weather Plus Network. She forecasted and presented the weather for NBC Weather Plus, CNBC, CNBC World, Nightly News, MSNBC and the NBC affiliate in San Diego, KNSD.

Merwin served as the morning meteorologist for CNBC’s morning show Squawk Box. While on CNBC, she covered the devasting Midwest flood of 2008 and Hurricane Gustav and broadcasted live from the NASDAQ for the show, Fast Money.

Also, Britta served for KCRA in Sacramento, California, News12 New Jersey, and News 12 Long Island as a Weekend Morning Meteorologist after she departed from the left eastern seaboard.
