TUI-owned cruise launches brand new US trips including Florida and New York

Marella Cruises, which is owned by TUI, will be launching four new cruise holidays across the pond from summer 2021.

The cruise line is sailing to US locations which include New York, Charleston, Norfolk (Virginia), Tampa, Key West and Fort Lauderdale.

Other destinations which Marella Cruises is planning a trip to includes sun-soaked travel spots like the Bahamas.

All of the holidays will depart from Port Canaveral in Florida, with a choice of either seven or 14 nights.

There are four itineraries to choose from: American Dream, Big Apple Adventure, Floridan Favourites and Sunshine State and Sands.

From November 7, travellers will be able to book on the Marella Cruises website, which includes return flights from five UK airports to Florida in the package.

Here is what each cruise holiday includes:

American Dream: Depart from Port Canaveral, the cruise includes a visit to Charleston in South Carolina, New York and Freeport in the Bahamas.

Travel

Big Apple Adventure: Depart from Port Canaveral, the cruise includes a visit to New York, Norfolk (Virginia) and Freeport in the Bahamas.

Floridian Favourites: Depart from Port Canaveral, the cruise includes a visit to Tampa and Fort Lauderdale in Florida, as well as Nassau in the Bahamas.

Sunshine State and Sands: Depart from Port Canaveral, the cruise includes a visit to Amber Cove in the Dominican Republic, Grand Turk in Turks and Caicos, as well as Key West and Miami in Florida.

The new line-up will also see the launch of a repositioning cruise, a 12-night Sunshine and Jazz itinerary which includes three days in New Orleans to explore its jazz scene.

The cruise will call at Montego Bay in Jamaica, Georgetown in Grand Cayman, New Orleans in Louisiana, as well as Tampa, Key West in Miami and Port Canaveral in Florida.
