Aaron Tippin Announces North American Tour Dates

NASHVILLE, TN (CelebrityAccess) — Country music performer Aaron Tippin announced that he's hitting the road in 2017.

Tippin announced 50 live shows in the first round of announcements and plans to release additional scheduled dates through the year.

The tour starts on Feb. 9th with a show at Northern Quest Casino in Airway Heights WA. with additional dates across the U.S. before Tipping wraps things up with an Oct. 28th show at Springfield Fall Festival in Springfield, Georgia. U.S. dates include concerts in Tampa, Nashville, Panama City Beach, Fort Myers, Bismarck, and Juneau.

Tippin's latest release is a two-disc retrospective of his back catalog features 10 newly recorded revisits of some of his big hits, as well as 15 news songs.
