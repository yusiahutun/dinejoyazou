Curated Swimsuits for Every Destination

It’s time to set your status to ‘out-of-office.’ Your quest for the best vacation swimwear officially ends here.

I've traveled to several dreamy resort getaways and found that each one has a specific swimwear vibe—which makes packing for an upcoming tropical escape that much easier. Ahead, I’ve curated the perfect swimwear labels for a variety of must-visit destinations. A dreamlike trip to the Four Seasons Anguilla pairs beautifully with romantic white bikinis from Magda Butrym; a weekend visit to Miami isn't complete without bold, vibrant hues from CIN CIN swim in your carry-on. There's plenty more where those came from, so get into relaxation mode with the best vacation swimwear for every luxurious trip you're taking this year below.

Vibrant Hues for Miami

Alicia Banilivy

The temperatures are hot and the style is even hotter. This is the place to wear your most glamorous and vibrant ensembles. Let Miami’s famous art deco buildings and their zest for living life colorfully inspire your swimwear. Go vibrant or go home. (Well, don't actually go home.)

Shop Vibrant Swim for Miami

Romantic Whites for Anguilla

Alicia Banilivy

Anguilla is perhaps one of the most romantic and beautiful destinations to visit—specifically if you're staying at the The Four Seasons. Whether you're a bride-to-be, baby-mooning, celebrating an anniversary, or just in need of a luxe getaway, this is the spot. Dreamy white swimwear sets the tone for a whimsical, relaxing vacation.

Shop White Swimsuits for Anguilla

Earthy Tones for Bora Bora

Alicia Banilivy

Earth tones don't have to be boring. Take your neutrals to the next level with rich and ultra chic tones for Bora Bora. These are timeless hues that complement every skin tone and coordinate with the island's natural beauty.

Shop Earth Tone Swim for Bora Bora

Shimmers in St. Barts

Alicia Banilivy

Sparkle and shine in St. Barts? Yes, please. Shimmer will not only make you stand out from the crowd but also enhance your glowy skin. With the styles below, all eyes will be on you while you’re catching those rays on the Caribbean island's equally glittery beaches.

Shop Shimmery Swim for St. Barts

Matching Sets for Tulum

Alicia Banilivy

Tulum is such a fun destination, whether you're traveling for a bachelorette or a romantic getaway. Matching sets are the way for days packed with activities and nights spent at the beachside bars. Grab a printed swimsuit and pareo from Agua by Agua Bendita or a robe set from Verdelimon and off you go. As for where to stay? Be Tulum or Nômade are my top recommendations for group or solo trips. Again, thank me later.

Shop Matching Sets for Tulum

Flaming Reds for Baha Mar

Alicia Banilivy

Bright red is the color trend of the season, after all, and this zesty shade matches Baha Mar's fun-loving energy. Travel trip: stay at the SLS.

Shop Red Swim for Baha Mar
