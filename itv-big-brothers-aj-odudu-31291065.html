ITV Big Brother's AJ Odudu wows fans with hair transformation in plunging jumpsuit

Big Brother host AJ Odudu has wowed fans with her latest glam hair transformation.

The 35-year-old presenter slipped her famous figure into a plunging jumpsuit for her latest hosting stint on ITV2 spin-off show Live & Live and looked stunning as ever as she debuted her brand new hairstyle with trendy blonde highlights.

AJ often changes up her look and her latest image overhaul went down a storm with fans watching at home. Taking to X, formerly known as Twitter, one fan penned: "AJ is always gonna serveee #BBLL #BBUK a beauty." Another added: "AJ’s hair game never slips. #BBUK #BBLL." A third person said: "Loving AJ’s hair tonight #BBUK #BBLL." Someone else posted: "AJ’s looking amazing tonight."

She wore her black hair down and wavy pairing the red attire with warm toned and bronzed eye makeup. One person on X said at the time, "Sorry but AJ Odudu is the most stunning person on earth." Another added: "My highlight of every evening is seeing what AJ Odudu is wearing on Big Brother."

Her latest show-stopping outfit comes days after AJ discussed whether she would consider taking over Holly Willoughby’s job on This Morning. The former presenter recently announced she would be stepping down from the ITV show after 14 years.

However, AJ shut down the idea citing the early starts as the main reason admitting that she "doesn’t do mornings." She is currently on-air late as the host of Big Brother: Late and Live so not wanting to be up at the crack of dawn is understandable.

AJ previously admitted that she felt the pressure following in the footsteps of Davina McCall, Brian Dowling and Emma Willis with co-host Will Best. She told Daily Star before the series kicked off: "The pressure is on, it's a big show, it's everyone's baby. We all grew up watching it but then there's a whole new audience that we want to bring along on the ride as well. And also it's had previous presenters who both Will and I both love, adore and respect, they're the nation's sweethearts, and we want to do everyone proud.

"So yeah, while the pressure is on and that can be very daunting, it's definitely counter-balanced by the fact that I'm presenting alongside Will. He's really good at his job and I'll be in safe hands with him and vice versa, so I'm actually more excited than nervous for sure."

"I definitely want to be myself and Will wants to be himself, we're not trying to mimic or imitate previous presenters that have been before. But it's really nice when you have a lot of good will and positive energy on your side for sure."
