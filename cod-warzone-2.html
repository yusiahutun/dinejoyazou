Robin Hood | DMZ Missions Guide in Warzone

Robin Hood is a DMZ Mission for the faction White Lotus featured in the DMZ mode of Call of Duty: Warzone 2.

In this guide, you can find all the mission's objectives and the rewards you'll obtain by completing it.

Robin Hood Mission Info

  • Rewards Sattiq Poppy Farmer House Key
    +10.000 XP

Robin Hood Mission Tasks:

  • Open 3 safes in one deployment
  • Extract 100k on a single teammate in one deployment

You will need the Sattiq Poppy Farmer House Key to complete the Espionage mission for Black Mous (Tier 2).
