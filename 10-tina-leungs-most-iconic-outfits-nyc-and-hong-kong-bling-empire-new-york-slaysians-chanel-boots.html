10 of Tina Leungs most iconic outfits in NYC and Hong Kong: from the Bling Empire: New York Slays

We delve into Leung’s love of the two cities and how she shows off different fashion styles in each …

NYC – conquering the concrete jungle

The Big Apple is undoubtedly one of the fashion capitals of the world. And when you’re crossing paths with Bella Hadid, Hailey Bieber, the Olsen Twins and Irina Shayk while grabbing an almond latte at Starbucks, you can bet it isn’t easy to make your mark when it comes to setting style trends.But that’s exactly what Bling Empire: New York star Leung, 40, has managed to achieve over the past decade. As part of the original group of Instagram influencers, Leung’s eclectic taste in fashion, bold looks on the red carpet and close friendships with well-known designers, have all come together to make the Hong Kong-born star an NYC fave.

Whether donning glitzy day looks while waiting for a cab or a patchwork jersey at a fancy event, her looks not only make her stand out in the city, but are also crowd-pleasers.

The Gucci-loving influencer has said that the Big Apple allows her to be different. “I’m a little more kooky in New York. I feel like I can let it all out and make however many mistakes I want,” she told Fashionista.

It’s this ability to be brave in fashion that has seen her evolve and work with top brands and designers.

Here are a few of her looks in NYC that have sent fans scrambling to double-tap on Instagram, pin on their Pinterest boards and reshare on their fashion blogs.

1. Marc Jacobs

The fashionista attended Marc Jacobs’ Perfect Party at Hotel Chelsea in December 2022. She showed her appreciation for the designer by wearing a look from his stand-out autumn/winter 2022 collection, per Hong Kong media.

2. Mugler

Leung wore a Mugler cut-out catsuit designed by Casey Cadwallader for the opening of the “Thierry Mugler: Couturissime” exhibition. The event was held at the Brooklyn Museum in November 2022.

3. Jimmy Choo

Who needs trainers to walk your dog in NYC? Here, Leung is wearing Jimmy Choo metallic knee-high boots. Because #Slaysian. She was walking the streets of NYC in November 2022 with her pal Laura Kim.

4. Peter Do

The Vietnamese-American designer is known for his minimalist looks and Leung paired one of his oversized coats perfectly with trousers and a sports bra in September 2022.

5. Area

New York fashion label Area got all the love when Leung wore a flared denim dress from its range. Throw in a Hello Kitty bag and the influencer again showed her versatility. Next to a quintessential brick-and-stone block of flats in NYC, Leung created the perfect scene for her snap posted in September 2022.

Hong Kong – where the magic began

Leung has enjoyed an ongoing love affair with Hong Kong. It was where she was born, after all. She left when she was two, then returned at age 11 and later got her first big break as a writer and stylist in the city. Some would say that Hong Kong incubated much of her talent.

As previously reported, she’s told the story of how, one day, she was at a salon having her hair done when her stylist introduced her to a man who had started a magazine. That man was Gordon Lam from Prestige. She landed a job and soon her blog Tina Loves garnered a cult following. She said the job caught her by surprise and that she had “no intention of staying in Hong Kong”.

Leung has describes her Hong Kong style to Fashionista as more “ladylike”. She told Monica Vinader that she loves visiting Star Street in Wan Chai because of its “cute” and “hippie” feel. According to Leung, local style can be summed up as “modern, practical and adaptable”.

Here are some of her best looks in Hong Kong.

6. Celine ft Chanel

Pairing Celine with Chanel, Leung added glam to this day look posted on her Instagram. She was visiting Hong Kong in 2018 and clearly pulled out all the stops with her style.

7. Bally

Leung went for a light and flowy feel when she wore this head-to-toe ensemble from the luxury Swiss fashion house. The outfit was part of the spring/summer 2019 collection and Leung wore it to the BallyxSwizzxShok1HK event, as seen on her Instagram.

8. Michelle Mason and Chanel

Wearing all black while visiting the city, Leung paired Michelle Mason with classic Chanel. In her Instagram caption of her photo, she said the items were currently on rotation from her wardrobe. Leung was at luxury hotel The Upper House in Admiralty.

9. Anya Hindmarch

This silver clutch from accessory designer Anya Hindmarch was the perfect statement piece for Leung’s casual outfit of the day. Hindmarch is known for her groundbreaking work in sustainability and Leung appears to count the brand as a firm favourite, pairing the bags with her outfits on the regular.

10. Esprit

Leung brought “teen spirit” with full 90s vibes for this designer collab with Esprit, as she mentions on her Instagram. She donned the outfit for an impromptu shoot in Happy Valley back in 2017.
