Emma Kenney says getting a dog from a breeder or pet store isn't just "Shameless," it's wrong.

The 17-year-old actress stars in PETA's new "video rant" against picking up a pooch anywhere but your local animal shelter.

"Opening up your life to a dog who needs a home is one of the most fulfilling things you can do," the teenager says in the clip (above). "You say you want a puppy who's a 'purebred'? Well, you know what's pure? My hatred for puppy mills."
