34 Facts about the movie Sister Act

Source: Disneyplus.com

Sister Act is a beloved musical comedy film that captured the hearts of audiences when it was released in 1992. Starring the incomparable Whoopi Goldberg, the movie tells the story of Deloris Van Cartier, a lively lounge singer who witnesses a murder and is placed in a witness protection program disguised as a convent nun. Directed by Emile Ardolino, Sister Act became an instant hit, grossing over $230 million worldwide. The film’s success can be attributed to its catchy music, hilarious comedy, and the unforgettable performance by Goldberg. In this article, we will delve into 34 fascinating facts about Sister Act that will not only entertain fans of the movie but also provide interesting insights into its production, cast, and legacy.
