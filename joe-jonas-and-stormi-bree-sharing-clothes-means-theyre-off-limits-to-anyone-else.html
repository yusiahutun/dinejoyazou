Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree sharing clothes means they're 'off limits' to anyone else

Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree have been spotted out once again, this time on the streets of New York City. Although they haven’t confirmed their relationship status, they’ve now got to the stage of sharing each other’s wardrobes, and fans think it’s adorable.

The rumored celebrity couple first sparked suspicions at the beginning of the year, when they were spotted leaving an airport in Cabo, Mexico together. Since then they’ve been spotted on numerous outings, but Joe Jonas seems to be wanting to keep the ‘relationship’ on the down low, five months after his divorce from Sophie Turner.

Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree are ‘off limits’

The pair were spotted on a stroll in the streets of New York, both wearing similar outfits, baggy bomber jackets, denim jeans, and matching black caps.

Eagle-eyed fans noticed that the jacket the model was wearing had actually been seen on the music star not long before, and it’s not the first time they’ve ‘shared clothes’.

As the two took a ‘romantic trip’ to Aspen, eagle-eyed fans noticed that the beanie she was wearing on her story actually belonged to Joe.

Fans have been ‘shipping’ the couple ever since the rumors emerged, and their fashion choices have fans shipping them even more.

“They’re like the same person! I’m so happy for Joe!” exclaimed one.

“Their style is similar, good for them,” wrote another.

Lending partner your jacket or clothes is said to “send signals to other guys that you’re off limits,” according to HuffPost.

“Not only does this show his willingness to make you comfortable, it also sends signals to other guys that you’re off limits. Lending you a jacket also means that he has to stay and reclaim the jacket when you leave, giving him a reason to stay close.”
