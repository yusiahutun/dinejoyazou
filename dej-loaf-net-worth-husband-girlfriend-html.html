DeJ Loaf Clears the Air Regarding Her Sexuality &amp; Dating Rumors

EntertainmentBy Asmita Neupane | On: June 29, 2022

American rapper Dej Loaf speaks out on her sexuality and boyfriend rumors that remained the talk of the town for quite a while. Having soared to popularity for her headbanger rap songs, the rapper's net worth to her personal life are all a matter of interest to her fans.

While her sexuality and questions about her boyfriend had been one of the most talked-about topics amongst her fanbase, she finally cleared the air talking about it in an interview with VLAD TV. 

Is Dej Loaf Gay?

Before addressing the rumors regarding her sexuality in the interview, the rapper mentioned she was not a lesbian through her Twitter post. 

Pin-pointing the same Twitter post, the interviewer asked the rapper to clarify the lesbian gossip circulating Dej for a long time. Her response went like this,

It's true, I am not a... or whatever people keep saying. I am none of that.

Dej Loaf, in an interview addressing the rumors about her sexuality (Source: YouTube)

Further, the interviewer addressed Dej's loose-baggy style apparel that had many speculate about her sexuality (absurd stereotypical mentality!).

However, the Try Me rapper has a different opinion about her choice of clothes. Responding to the interviewer's question regarding her apparel in her music video for Try Me, where she transitions from wearing only intimates to putting on baggy clothes, she said,

Sexy is what you make it. If you go back, everybody used to wear baggy clothes and it's just comfortable. I like to dress comfortably...I don't worry too much about that.

Romantic Life with a Boyfriend?

Though the rapper confirmed her sexuality as straight, her relationship with her reported former girlfriend, Aye Redd, sheds light on a different story.

Dej hit several tabloid headlines in the past when her 'secret' ex-girlfriend bashed their hidden romance through Instagram, writing,

"I guess the new thing now is to fake it until you make it. Fake what you got. Fake who you in love with. Fake your character. Fake your pride. Fake what you believe in because of what others might perceive you as. I just can't go out like that. I can only be me. Not hiding."

At the same time, her rumor of dating rapper and alleged boyfriend Lil Durk, with whom she collaborated on a few songs, also grabbed massive buzz from the media. But, debunking her boyfriend rumors, she spilled the tea in an interview with HOT 97, saying none of those ever happened. In her words,

I'm single. I've been single for quite some time now. it's pretty much BS. Like, I'm pretty much single and that's all that really matters. Nothing really matters. (Jokingly says) Nobody deserves me, I'm chillin'.

As she has never publicly confirmed having a husband or being in any romantic relationship with a boyfriend, it can be said she is probably single. Moreover, her social media presence also does not hint at anything affirming regarding her love life.

While the rapper's mysterious yet not so mysterious love life is seemingly on a halt, her career, on the other hand, is running miles blessing her bank account with a hefty net worth.

Net Worth

The Detroit-born DeJ Loaf, age 30, embarked on the world of fame as a Hip Hop artist in 2011. A couple of years later, she released her first mixtape, Just Do It, which was quick to turn many heads.

Seeing her growing potential in the music industry, she was later signed by the IBGM record label; soon after, she released her breakthrough single, Try Me.

The blowup song released in 2014 now has over 100 million viewers on YouTube. Following her hit, she went on to have one studio album Sell Sole II, under her belt, along with four extended plays.

By now, you might have figured out she is rich. Celebrity Net Worth reports that the rapper's net worth as of 2021 comes to 2 million dollars.

While the rapper is still on the roll, giving out more of her music, her bank account will seemingly get fatter in the coming years.
