Trending Now In Our Store: Roman Holiday

courtesy

Because all that's old is new again, this summer we're taking Audrey Hepburn's character Princess Anne in the 1953 classic film, Roman Holiday, as our style muse. Think: a chic white shirt tucked into a full-circle skirt, finished with a necktie and a pulled-back hairdo—BYO yourself a Gregory Peck and gelato. Our go-to designers encapsulate the feminine vibe of the look with a modern twist: Lisa Marie Fernandez, Bliss and Mischief, and, of course, the Italians Dolce & Gabanna. Si, si. 

For more of the trend we're loving this week, head to our Trending Now shopping guide on ShopBAZAAR.
