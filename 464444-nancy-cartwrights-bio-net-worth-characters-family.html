After her high school graduation, Nancy earned a scholarship to Ohio University. She participated in public speaking competitions and emerged the winner in most of them while at the institution. In 1976, she was hired to do voice-overs for advertisements on the Dayton radio station WING. Later, she was hired by Warner Bros.

Later, she left Ohio for Los Angeles after Daw Butler became her mentor. Consequently, she transferred from Ohio University and joined the University of California, Los Angeles (UCLA). She graduated in 1981 with a degree in theatre.
