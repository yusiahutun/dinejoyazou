The Nanny fans, rejoice!

The show's stars, Charles Shaughnessy, Fran Drescher and Renée Taylor had a mini cast reunion last week — and the pics of them together again are taking us back to the 1990s!

"An evening with the mother-in-law! Happy Birthday, Renée!," Charles wrote alongside a snapshot of himself with Renée and Fran on Facebook. The trio met at Fran's house to celebrate Renée's 83rd birthday on Saturday, March 19.

He later shared another pic of himself happily hugging Fran. Since the show ended in 1999, Charles has guest-starred on her hit TV Land series, Happily Divorced.
