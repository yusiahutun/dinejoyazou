A personal goal of mine this year is to upgrade my style in any way I can; and spring is the perfect opportunity to do so. My favorite part of a new season rolling around is the endless stream of new fashion options—I mean what could be better than a flowy new romper or a cute little going out top? Amazon thankfully is filled with fresh spring fashion that is so chic, I’ll be buying all I can.

My newest rule of thumb when shopping is buying some staple pieces I know I will wear on repeat. This way, I can justify my purchases and know that I will be getting the most bang for my buck. In an attempt to channel my inner Rachel Berry, I want to look as adorable as possible during springtime in NYC.
