Stable Ronaldo (real name – Rani Netz) is an American online gamer and Twitch star known for his mastery of the Fortnite streaming game and is signed with FaZe Clan.

The Origin Story

Built By Gamers

Not too long after this spike in fame, he gained notoriety as a skilled online gamer which led him to be signed by Built By Gamers.

NRG eSports


Just a few short months after being signed with Built By Gamers, Rani was approached by none other than NRG eSports.

NRG Esports is a leading esports organization based in Los Angeles, California. It boasts a roster of popular games such as Call of Duty, Valorant, Fortnite, and Overwatch, among many others.

In addition to this, they are known for recruiting top, young, emerging gaming talent, and Ronaldo happened to be one of their picks in 2020.
