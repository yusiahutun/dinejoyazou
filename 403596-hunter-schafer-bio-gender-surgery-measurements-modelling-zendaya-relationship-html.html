Is Hunter Schafer transgender?

In an interview, Schafer said that she identifies as a trans person and that she wanted people to know she was not a cis girl because it is not something she is or feels like she is. She went on to say she was proud of being a trans person and has been among the active voices for the rights of transgender people.

Cisgender is a term used to refer to someone whose current gender is the same one that was presumed of them at birth.
