James Marsden divorce: Lisa Linde and X Men star enjoy family day out with son Jack

Happy families: James Marsden and wife Lisa Linde enjoy day out with their son... less than two weeks after she filed for divorce

By Sarah Fitzmaurice
Updated: 09:29 EDT, 3 October 2011

6

She might have filed for divorce less than two weeks ago but it seems that Lisa Linde and her husband James Marsden are still one happy family.

The X Men star and his wife were seen enjoying a family day out with their son Jack over the weekend.

James,38,  and Lisa, couldn’t have looked less like a couple undergoing a divorce as they swung their son in the arms together.

Happy family: Despite filing for divorce less than two weeks ago Lisa Linde and her husband James Marsden appear to be on extremely good terms as they enjoyed a day out with their son over the weekend

The family looked happy and relaxed as they shared a joked together strolling in the sunshine on Saturday.

Lisa was dressed in a pair of light denim dungarees which she had rolled up, with just a black vest underneath.

James, also opted for a dressed down look in a pair of jeans a white T-shirt and a baseball cap for the family outing.

Good friends: The couple shared a joke together and with their son Jack as they enjoyed a relaxed Saturday in the sunshine

Close: After it emerged that Linde had filed for divorce a spokesperson for the couple said it was a mutual decision and they would remain good friends

While the family looked the epitome of happy over the weekend Lisa filed for divorce on September 23 cited irreconcilable differences in the papers.

A representative for the 38-year-old actor told TMZ: 'This was a mutual decision. They are committed to raising their kids together and remain great friends.'

The couple have been married since July 22, 2000 and have two children together, a son and a daughter.

In happier times: The couple, seen in 2010, are ending their 11-year marriage

The website reports that they have video of the Straw Dogs star on the night his wife filed her divorce papers, where he did not appear upset.

In fact, they caught him at 4:30 in the morning outside of the Papaya King hot dog shop in New York, singing with a group of friends.

The last time the couple appeared publicly together was on March 28  t the LA premiere of Marsden's Easter film, Hop.

They were last snapped by photographers having lunch together a week later in LA's Brentwood neighbourhood.

James is currently shooting the comedy film Bachelorette with Kirsten Dunst.
