Kanye West fans think wife Bianca looks like Kim Kardashian and it's creeping them out

Kanye West’s relationship with his wife Bianca Censori has been scrutinized, especially when it comes to her clothing choices since they became a couple. However, fans have noticed something “weird” about the architect.

Kanye West began dating Bianca soon after his divorce from Kim Kardashian in 2022. They quickly tied the knot that October. Their relationship has fascinated people on social media such as X (formerly Twitter), and her shockingly X-rated outfits have often caught the public’s attention. But now fans are noticing other details about her and making them uncomfortable.

Fans notice something ‘weird’ about Kanye’s wife Bianca

After NSFW pictures were posted of the 28-year-old, a now pretty common occurrence, people couldn’t help but notice Bianca’s resemblance to Kim, 43. They joked she was like a “drawing” of the reality TV star.

Kanye initially posted the photos on his Instagram. This isn’t common for the star, as Kanye West usually stays clear of social media for months at a time. But he’s clearly had a change of heart recently.

One person wrote: “She’s like if someone tried drawing Kim K from memory,” and someone else echoed, “This lit just another Kim K.”

Another penned: “The fact she looks so much like Kim is kinda weird,” and added a laughing face emoji.

A third joked: “Mom: ‘We have Kim K at home’ *The Kim K at home*.”

Bianca’s friends are ‘concerned’ about her

A source previously told US Weekly Kanye West’s wife Bianca feels “isolated” in their relationship and the Australian native’s friends find it “hard to tell” how she’s feeling, but they are “happy if she’s happy.”

West “hasn’t found this kind of happiness in a long time,” they said. “Bianca fulfills him in so many ways and understands him like nobody else ever has.
