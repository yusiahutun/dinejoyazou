Here is the New Feature of Instagram: Limits

As a result of the negative situations often encountered on Instagram, Instagram has decided to take measures in this regard. In order to prevent the disturbing comments and DMs that users frequently encounter, Instagram announced a setting called “Limits”. Limits allow users to hide unwanted comments and DMs for a while. To use this setting, users can go to the Privacy tab in the settings. In here, there is “Limits”. Users can select who to limit in this section. They can choose to limit the accounts that aren’t following them or they can choose recent followers. After choosing whom to limit, users can now choose for how long they want to limit. They can limit an account from 1 day to 1 month. Thus, users will be able to continue to enjoy Instagram without any discomfort.
