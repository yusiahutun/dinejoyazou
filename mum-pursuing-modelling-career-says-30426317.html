Mum pursuing modelling career says her kids fully back her despite saucy lingerie pics

A mum has spoken out after her saucy lingerie pics and modelling career have seen her reach the finals of a top competition, and has claimed her children approve of her career choice.

Kylie Gill, 34, raised eyebrows when she became a finalist of Top Model 2023 in London – despite having three kids.

The full-time model has had a rough journey to the top, having cared for two of her children by the age of 18, with a third by the time she was 21, Surrey Live reports.
