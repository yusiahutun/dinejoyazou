Scarlett Johansson and Colin Jost in Armani at White House

Scarlett Johansson and Colin Jost are nailing the White House edition of date night. After twinning in matching menswear-inspired looks one day earlier at a White House Correspondents' Dinner kickoff party, the couple upped the glam for the annual star-studded fundraiser in Washington, D.C. The actress and the Saturday Night Live comedian – who was the featured entertainer at this year’s event – were all smiles as they coordinated in their best classic Hollywood ‘fits on Saturday night.

The Black Widow star lit up the red carpet in a strapless white gown from Armani Privé, which had its bodice and part of the skirt lined with a string of iridescent crystals. Draping a matching cream chiffon scarf around her neck, Johansson complemented her dress’s blinged-out accents with statement diamond earrings and a tennis bracelet.

The Outset founder, who could be seen smiling and gazing at her husband as they were photographed on the red carpet, wore her bombshell waves in a sleek, center-parted style tucked behind her ears. She chose sultry cat eyes and pink blush, and swiped on a more warm-toned version of her signature cherry red lipstick for the occasion.

Meanwhile, Jost looked dapper for his hosting duties in a satin-edged navy blue Giorgio Armani tux paired with a white shirt and a black bow tie.

Their couple aesthetic was a complete 180 from the power suits they sported the night before at the CAA Kickoff Party at La Grande Boucherie. The theme of the day was menswear, as Johanssen went for a modern charcoal suit which she styled without a top underneath. The comedian did a more casual take, wearing a navy blue suit and leaving his white shirt unbuttoned.
