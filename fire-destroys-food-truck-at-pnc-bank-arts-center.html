Fire Destroys Food Truck At PNC Bank Arts Center

HOLMDEL, NJ (CelebrityAccess) — A fire destroyed a food truck at PNC Bank Arts Center in Holmdel, NJ at the start of a busy weekend at the venue.

According to NJ Advance Media, the fire, which broke out in a shish-kebob food truck, was contained by firefighters and did not put any other areas of the venue at risk.

Lt. Brian Polite, a spokesman for the New Jersey State Police told Advance Media that state police received a call about the fire at approximately 5:30 AM.

Members of the State Police arson unit investigated the fire, but found nothing suspicious, Polite said and preliminary findings suggest that the fire may have stemmed from propane tank.

The fire occurred hours before Keith Urban was set to perform at the venue. As well, the Vans Warped Tour was set to land at the venue on Sunday. – Staff Writers
