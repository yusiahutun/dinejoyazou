About Hospital Hill High School

When was Hospital Hill High School started? Hospital Hill High School was established in 1977 with its first students, mainly Asians, Whites, and affluent Africans. The government took over the school in 1983 as a mixed-day school and later changed to boarding.

Is Hospital Hill High School mixed? Hospital Hill High School is not a mixed school. In 2015, the girls were faced out, and the school upgraded to an extra county boys' boarding school. It operates under the 8-4-4 curriculum approved by the Ministry of Education. Just like other schools, the facility partakes in the quota system of admission such that students are drawn from every part of Kenya.

Hospital Hill High School location

Hospital Hill High School is a boys-only boarding secondary school that has been in existence for more than four decades. It is located in Highridge in Westland constituency, Nairobi County. The school was initially located at the present-day Hospital Hill primary school in Parklands, Nairobi. It was later moved to the present site in Nyari Estate in 1979 as a private mixed-day secondary school.

Hospital Hill High School KCSE performance

Hospital Hill High School has been known for its academic and sports excellence since its inception. 2019 was its best-performing year. The school posted good results to rank among the best-performing schools in Nairobi County. Unfortunately, the institution has not been posting its KCSE results online.
