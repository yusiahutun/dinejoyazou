10 trendy half up half down hairstyles with weaves to try out

Are you looking for a hairstyle that will draw attention by highlighting your best facial features? If yes, try the trendy half up half down hairstyles. They are versatile and incredibly flattering, giving you the best of both worlds.

Half up half down hairstyles with weaves are popular and characterised by some hair being styled or secured upward while leaving the remaining portion of the hair to flow down freely. The weave extensions offer an exciting twist to the hairstyles by enhancing the hair texture, colour, length and volume.

Trendy half up half down hairstyles with weaves

Are you looking to try quick weave hairstyles half up half down that are steadily becoming popular? According to Sister’s Bombshell these are the trendy half up half down hairstyles with weaves to choose from.

1. Half up, half down quick weave with fringe

This trendy hairstyle includes different elements to create an eye-catching look. The fringe can be customised to suit your face shape and style preferences, ranging from side-swept to straight-across bangs or layered fringe. In addition, the hairstyle is suitable for various occasions.

2. Half up half down weave with curls

The half up half down weave with curls is a trendy and glamorous hairstyle that'll give you a sophisticated look. It balances the updo and free-flowing curls, making it great for any event. 3. Accessorised half up down hairstyles with weaves

This hairstyle is elegant and trendy. The weaves add length and volume, creating a luxurious appearance. Accessorising the hairstyle with embellishments such as jewelled pins, metallic hair clips, and delicate flowers enhances the overall look.

4. Half up half down with braids in the front

At the forefront, the intricately woven braids beautifully frame the face. The attached braids then seamlessly flow down the back, creating a sophisticated and casual allure. This hairstyle is ideal for different occasions as it offers a modern twist of a classic look while maintaining its stylish demeanour.

5. Half up half down weave with colour

This hairstyle is trendy and playful. You can incorporate any bold hue into the weave to make it vibrant and pop with personality. Ensure the top layers are elegantly gathered to make it a standout.

6. Smooth ponytail half up half down weave

Integrating a beautiful ponytail with a half up half down quick weave isn't only natural and adorable. You can achieve this polished look by gathering your upper hair layers into a high ponytail and attaching high-quality weave for sleekness. 7. Half up half down hairstyles with weaves for straight hair

This hairstyle exudes sophistication; it consists of smooth, straight weaves flowing down and elegantly gathered upper hair layers. The straight weaves add a glossy finish, making it an excellent hairstyle for various high-end events.
