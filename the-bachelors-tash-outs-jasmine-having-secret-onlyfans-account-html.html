The Bachelors: Tash outs Jasmine for having a secret OnlyFans account

The Bachelors has been rocked by a MAFS-inspired scandal after 'villain' Tash Candyce outed Jasmine Absolom as a secret OnlyFans model.

Tash, 31, announced on Wednesday's episode that Jasmine had an OnlyFans account and questioned whether the 24-year-old was on the show 'for the right reasons'.

She told the other women about her love rival's racy side-hustle while Jasmine was speaking privately to suitor Jed McIntosh.

'I didn't say anything about Jasmine's OnlyFans at all to Jed because that's not my place. That's not of my business,' Tash said snarkily. 

While it's unclear why Tash decided to bring it up at all, she did hint at a feud between the pair in a piece-to-camera interview.
