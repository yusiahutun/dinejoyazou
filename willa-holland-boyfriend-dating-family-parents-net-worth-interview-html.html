Willa Holland Is Dating! Has A Low-key Relation With Boyfriend

EntertainmentBy LRU Writer | On: March 18, 2018

The top child model of the USA during her childhood, actress Willa Holland is not new to the limelight, as she has gained popularity with the role of Kaitlin Cooper in the FOX Teen drama, The O.C.

Meanwhile, as prosperous her career is, Willa's love life matches the same amount of fortune. Want to know who makes Willa fortunate by showering his love on her? Keep reading!

Willa Holland's Dating Life

Also known as the voice actor, Willa Holland shares her romantic time with the Los Angeles native photographer Nate Walton. The exact date of their dating relationship was not known until she uploaded the duo's image on Instagram, back in November 2015.

The actress was seen giving a romantic kiss to her boyfriend in public, which confirmed their romantic union. 

Willa Holland goes public regarding her bond with Nate. (Photo: Willa Holland's Instagram)

They did not shy away from flaunting their adorable pictures on the social networking sites further. Back in October 2016, Willa uploaded an image on her Instagram account where she was enjoying sleeping on her boyfriend's arm accompanied by their dog.

Moreover, with their love stepping into the next year, 2017; Nate seemed more active in flaunting their romantic life out to the viewers and their fans. He shared his feelings toward Willa through an Instagram photo with the caption, "love you.. thank you for everything you do."

After reaching two years milestone in their relationship, Nate took to Instagram to express his love while stating that he is renewing their relationship for the third season. 

(Photo: Nate Walton's Instagram)

They indeed made it to 2018 together and hopefully; the bond remains intact forever and after.

Meanwhile, talking about her previous romantic involvements, Willa was indulged into the dating rumors with her former on-screen partner of the series, Arrow, Colton Haynes in 2016.

Colton shared a photo of Holland on his Instagram in which they were kissing. The picture followed the caption, "What happens in the UK stays in the UK...or does it? ? #thearoy #arrow."

Moreover, Willa too had uploaded a photo on her Instagram back in Jul 2014 with Colton, Colin Donnell and the caption read, "Out for a stroll with my ex-boyfriend and dead half-brother." Well, though they hinted about their relationship somehow, Colton Haynes is gay in real life and has already exchanged vows with Jeff Leatham. So, Willa comes clear of the said rumor. 

Additionally, Willa as well was romantically linked with the British actor, Toby Hemingway in the past.

More About Willa Holland

 American actress Willa Holland was born on 18th June 1991 to the cinematographer father, Keith Holland and an actress mother Darnell Gregorio-De Palma. In her family, she has an elder sister Brianna Holland and a younger half-sister, Piper De Palma.

With her parents in the film industry, she became the part of the series, The O.C at the young age of 14. In an interview with Wonderland,  Willa even mentioned that she could relate to her character in The O.C

“I think I was right there in that stage of life where Kaitlin was. It was very obvious to me why I got cast in that role at that time.”

Likewise, Holland has appeared in the TV series, which includes, The Comeback, The O.C., Gossip Girl, Arrow and The Flash. The actress with a net worth of $ 5 million has also appeared in many movies including, Garden Party, Genova, Legion, Straw Dogs, and Tiger Eyes among others.
