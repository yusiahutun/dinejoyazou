Bath &amp; Body Works Rolled Out Laundry Detergent &amp; Fans Are Losing It

Bath & Body Works has basically taken over our home. We have candles and plug-in air fresheners, body and hair products in the bathroom, and hand sanitizer in our bags. Now, Bath & Body Works is coming for your laundry. After selling out on the app-exclusive pre-launch, White Barn Fabric Care is back in stock online only. But we have a feeling, not for long.

The retailer rolled out two major products: Fabric Care Detergent and Fabric Care Fragrance Booster. The former “combines the science of stain fighting with perfumer-created fine fragrance,” per the brand, available in 14 scents you already know and love. There are also four totally new ones to make the chore of doing laundry a bit more enjoyable.

The concentrated formula washes up to 64 large loads and cleans well in cold water. The chic little bottles are made of 85 percent recycled plastic, too.

And if the laundry detergent scent isn’t enough for you, or if you’d rather use your own detergent and add a little boost, Bath & Body Works also launched Fabric Care Fragrance Boosters. They’re available in eight popular fragrances and four brand-new scents created just for this collection.

Reviews of the new products are very good, to say the least. One reviewer is such a fan of the Fabric Care Fragrance Boosters, that she wrote a whole paragraph: “Absolutely in love with this! I use with the detergent. I do have to add water to the detergent dispenser bc it’s so thick. I had tried everything to kill the musky smell of my husbands clothes & this is literally the only brand detergent that works! Plus, my house, closets, beds, & attire all smell amazing now!!!”

Another is a big fan of the Fabric Care Detergent in the popular candle scent, Champagne Toast. She wrote: “I bought this as launch for members and don’t regret it. I can’t wait until they release the full line because this sold out quick. I thought this fragrance was going to be strong because it is concentrated, but it wasn’t. Soft and subtle. Kept my clothes fresh for at least 14 days. I really use it on the towels.”

Hurry and grab your faves while you can.
