A Charlie Brown Christmas is the first TV special based on the comic strip, Peanuts, by Charles M. Schulz. The animated TV special, however, is produced by Lee Mendelson and directed by Bill Melendez. The program premiered on CBS on December 9, 1965 and tells the story of Charlie Brown feeling blue during the holiday season. When his friends finally come around, they help lift Charlie’s spirits so that they can all have a cheerful Christmas together. On top of its signature Peanuts illustrations and style, A Charlie Brown Christmas is known for its jazzy songs that make you want to join the characters in dance and celebration.

The holiday special has become a classic in its genre and has also inspired others of its kind and even live theatrical versions. Other TV specials featuring Peanuts characters include A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving, It’s the Great Pumpkin, Snoopy, Come Home, You’re Not Elected, Charlie Brown, and There’s No Time for Love, Charlie Brown, among others.

If you’re in the mood for a Christmas classic, you’re likely trying to figure out how you can watch A Charlie Brown Christmas with your friends an family. Keep scrolling to discover where to watch A Charlie Brown Christmas for free to see Charlie and his crew discover the true meaning of Christmas.

Where to watch A Charlie Brown Christmas for free

A Charlie Brown Christmas is available to stream on Apple TV Plus, which offers a seven-day free trial and costs $9.99 per month after the trial ends. Along with A Charlie Brown Christmas, Apple TV Plus also includes dozens of Apple Originals with new content add each month. Apple Originals include award-winning comedy and drama series such as Ted Lasso, Severance, The Morning Show and Bad Sisters, as well as original movies, documentaries, and children’s content. The service also offers other Peanuts specials such as A Charlie Brown Thanksgiving, It’s the Great Pumpkin, Charlie Brown, and Be My Valentine, Charlie Brown. Apple TV Plus additionally allows users to stream on up to five devices and download content to watch offline.

How to watch A Charlie Brown Christmas for free

Read on for step-by-step instructions on how to watch A Charlie Brown Christmas on Apple TV Plus.

  • Visit TV.Apple.com
  • Click “Try It Free“
  • Create an account with your email or Apple ID
  • Enter your information and payment method
  • Search for “A Charlie Brown Christmas” and start watching
