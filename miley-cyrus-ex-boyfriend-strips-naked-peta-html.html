Miley Cyrus' ex boyfriend strips naked for PETA

By Kelly Hartog
Updated: 20:11 EDT, 2 July 2010

View
comments


Miley Cyrus has been criticised for baring too much flesh, but she has well and truly been outdone by her ex boyfriend Justin Gaston.

The former underwear model, 21, has posed in his birthday suit for the animal rights activist group, PETA (People for the Ethical Treatment of Animals).

Miley and Justin, who split last year, were introduced by Miley's father, singer, Billy Ray Cyrus, when Justin appeared on the U.S. reality TV show, Nashville Star in 2008.

Baring it all: Justin Gaston (far right), Giglianne Braga and Ben Elliot pose for the PETA campaign

Although Justin was eliminated in week three, Billy Ray encouraged Justin to stick around and he was soon attending church with Miley and her family.

Billy Ray felt Justin was a nice, Christian boy and a good influence on his daughter, even though Miley was only 15 at the time and Justin was 19.

However, Miley and Justin broke up a year later amid rumours that Miley had dumped him for Nick Jonas of The Jonas Brothers.

Back in the day: Miley and Justin dated for a year from June 2008

Justin, who is also an aspiring musician, appears in the PETA campaign with Giglianne Braga, an aspiring model and Ben Elliot, an aspiring actor.

All three star in a new 24/7 reality show called If I Can Dream, on the U.S. website, Hulu.

The show follows five aspiring artists trying to break into Hollywood and is the brainchild of Simon Fuller of 19 Entertainment.

Dreaming of fame: The PETA campaign could launch the careers of these reality stars

Simon is responsible for the phenomenal success of both American Idol and So You Think You Can Dance.

Wearing nothing but a silver cross around his neck and a tattoo on his hip, Justin says that people should not be distracted by the racy nude photographs.

He said:'With this PETA campaign, what I really wanted was it not to be about me but to be about the animals. We're trying to bring light to this cause.'

PETA proponents: 'We're trying to bring light to this cause,' said Justin

Of course it doesn't hurt that the campaign is also providing publicity for the web series. The PETA poster also has a logo and the website link for the series.

PETA has a long history of campaigns starring naked celebrities.

Eva Mendes, Khloe Kardashian, Gisele Bundschen, Alicia Silverstone, Holly Madison and Paris Hilton are just a few stars that have stripped down for the organization.
