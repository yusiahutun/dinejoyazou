If you’ve never missed a game yet, you may be wondering how to watch the Rose Bowl live for free to see Alabama Crimson Tide and Michigan Wolverines face off. Keep reading for the best and one of the only ways to watch the Rose Game live for free this New Year’s Day.

The Rose Bowl Game is an annual American college football game traditionally played on January 1—New Year’s Day—at the Rose Bowl in Pasadena, California. The game, which has been nicknamed “The Granddaddy of Them All” by broadcaster Keith Jackson, started in 1902 and has been played annually since 1916. The Rose Bowl Game is also a part of the Pasadena Tournament of Roses Association’s “America’s New Year Celebration,” which also includes the historic Rose Parade.

Starting in 2015, the Rose Bowl has been a part of the College Football Playoff as one of the New Year’s Six bowls, which also includes the Orange Bowl, the Sugar Bowl, the Cotton Bowl, the Peach Bowl, and the Fiesta Bowl. The 2024 Rose Bowl Game will be played between the fourth ranked Alabama Crimson Tide of the SEC and the first ranked Michigan Wolverines of the Big Ten.
