Stuart Air Show to Return

Celebrating 30 years, the Stuart Air Show returns in 2020 with more incredible aerobatic performances than ever before. The Veteran’s Day weekend event will take place at Witham Field on November 7 and 8, and promises exhilarating shows from four elite U.S. Air Force fighter jet performers, plus more soaring performances from groups like the USASOC Black Daggers parachute team.

Not all of the action will be in the air; a freestyle motocross show will feature acclaimed FMX riders from across the country, including world-record holder Cody Elkins (the first person to jump over a flying airplane). 1895 Flying Fortress Lane, Stuart; advance tickets $20/adults, $5/children ages 5 and younger (upgrades like spectator seating and premium parking available online)
