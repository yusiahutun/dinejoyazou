Eddie Vedder &amp; Incubus To Headline 2019 Innings Festival

ARIZONA (CelebrityAccess) – Innings Festival announced this week that Eddie Vedder and Incubus will headline its 2019 event.

Scheduled to take place on March 2 and 3 in Tempe, Arizona, the festival lineup also includes performances by Sheryl Crow, Liz Phair, Jimmy Eat World, Band of Horses, Grouplove, St. Paul & The Broken Bones, Blues Traveler, DOROTHY, Shakey Graves, Cake, Guster, Black Pistol Fire, Roger Clyne & The Peacemaker, Mat Kearney and G. Love & Special Sauce.

As is always the case, the festival will also feature appearances by a number of MLB legends including Roger Clemens, Jake Peavy, Sean Casey, Huston Street and Eric Byrnes among others.

Tickets for Innings Festival are currently on sale to the general public and can be purchased at inningsfestival.com.
