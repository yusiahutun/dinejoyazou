Kevin Jonas Baby: Joe, Nick Jonas Congratulate Brother on Twitter

Kevin Jonas isn't the only one who "can't wait" to meet his first child with wife Danielle — the singer's brothers Joe and Nick Jonas are almost as thrilled as the dad-to-be himself. Both took to Twitter shortly after the July 9 baby announcement to send their well-wishes to the Married to Jonas stars.

"Can't explain how excited I am about this. I'm going to be an uncle! Congrats @kevinjonas & @daniellejonas!!" Joe, 23, tweeted. Later, he shared a funny picture of an ultrasound of a baby drinking from a Starbucks cup, captioning it, "So pumped to be an uncle."

Nick, meanwhile, wrote: "Can't wait to welcome the newest addition of the Jonas family. Congratulations to my bro."

As Us Weekly previously reported, Kevin announced on July 9 that he and his wife are expecting a baby later this year. "It's true — Danielle is pregnant! Can't even imagine how excited we are," the 25-year-old singer tweeted. "I can't wait to share this with my best friend. I love you baby."

Danielle, for her part, opened up about the couple's quest to become parents last August. "We're just waiting for the right time, and to have enough time to spend with the child and not miss everything about them growing up," she said. "I think that's our biggest concern."
