She’s nominated twice tonight, and Emma Stone’s Golden Globes 2024 look is already setting the tone for what’s bound to be an exciting awards season for the actress.

The Poor Things actress donned an elegant nude dress for her 2024 Golden Globes red carpet appearance. The gown, which you can check out below, featured delicate floral appliqué embellishments, a plunging neckline, and a high leg slit. She completed her look with a pair of strappy nude heels and silver dangling earrings.

Stone is in the running for multiple awards at the 2024 Golden Globes, notably Best Performance by an Actress in a Motion Picture, Musical or Comedy for her role in Poor Things, and Best Performance by an Actress in a Television Series, Drama for her work in The Curse.

Mark Ruffalo, who stars alongside Stone in Poor Things, recently expressed admiration for her performance at the New York City premiere of the film in an interview with People, calling her “a once in a generation talent.” He praised her fearlessness, describing her role as one of the most daring and compelling female performances in decades.

“She’s just so game. I mean, this is probably one of the most daring female performances in decades, in a sense,” the actor said. “It’s just the kind of fearlessness that she has and a presence, and I mean presence with you as an actor,” he continued. “She’s just right there, no matter what you do she goes with you. And she’s a great talent.”

Meanwhile, Stone recently shared her thoughts on her Hollywood journey at the Palm Springs Awards, where she received the prestigious Desert Palm Achievement Award. Addressing some “totally garbage” advice she received as a teenager, Stone dismissed sexist notions about aging in the industry. “When I first moved to L.A., I went to one of those general meetings that they sometimes send you to, and an executive told me that for male actors, it’s a marathon, not a sprint. And in his eyes, for women, it was a sprint, not a marathon. And that was 20 years ago,” she said, per People.

She continued, “I realize that advice is total garbage because the majority of the women that I look up to in this industry, many of whom are in this room, have proven that as time goes on, life and work only get more interesting and more fulfilling.”
