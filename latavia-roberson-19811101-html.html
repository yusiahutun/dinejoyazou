LaTavia Roberson (Pop Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

LaTavia Marie Roberson is an American singer, songwriter and actress. Roberson first rose to prominence in the late 1990s as the original member of R&B group Destiny's Child, one of the best-selling girl groups of all time. After leaving the group, Roberson founded Anjel with former Destiny's Child member LeToya Luckett. After revamping their album, Roberson took a break from work and returned to Houston. Roberson has performed in various plays including That Jeans, How to Love, and Not My Family. Roberson also became a major member of R&B Divas: Atlanta alongside singers like Angie Stone, Keke Wyatt and longtime friend Meelah of 702. Roberson has sold over 25 million records with Destiny's Child. His work has earned him numerous awards and nominations, including two Grammy Awards and three Soul Train Music Awards.
