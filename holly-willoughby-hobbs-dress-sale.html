Holly Willoughby wows in floral 139 Hobbs dress now in the sale!

The This Morning host looked fabulous in the store's 'Dale Dress'

    Holly Willoughby has long been a fan of the British high street, and it looks as if Hobbs is a particular favourite with the TV star right now. In the last two weeks, Holly has been spotted in three of the store's stylish dresses while presenting . Most recently on Wednesday, Holly turned heads in the £139 navy Dale Dress, which is described by the store as the "perfect work-to-weekend" piece.

    View post on Instagram 

    Holly Willoughby looked beautiful in a floral dress from Hobbs

    The design, which features a feminine silhouette, short bell sleeves and a ditzy floral print, looked fantastic on the 36-year-old. What's more, it is currently being sold with a 20% discount, costing £111.20 while on special offer.

    Holly teamed her dress with a pair of strappy black heels by Ego. Beauty-wise, the star wore her long, blonde hair down in loose waves and styled in a middle parting. Her make-up was kept natural, with a glowy base enhancing her complexion and a sweep of blusher across the apples of her cheeks. A smoky eye, statement brow and nude lipgloss completed her look.

    View post on Instagram 

    The TV star has been seen in a number of Hobbs dresses over the last few weeks

    Last Wednesday, Holly wore another stylish design by Hobbs, this time the £149 Beatrice Dress. The vibrant shirt dress was adorned with red, navy and white stripes, and featured a flattering waist tie and three quarter length roll-up sleeves.

    View post on Instagram 

    After Holly wore this Hobbs dress, it sold out in less than a day

    On Monday, meanwhile, Holly chose to wear Hobb's £169 Kayla dress, which featured a blue speckled print. The design proved to be so popular with the TV presenter's fans, that it sold out under a day after she posted a photo of her wearing it on Instagram. Talking to HELLO! Online, Hobbs revealed: "The Kayla Dress actually sold out in under a day when she wore it last week."
