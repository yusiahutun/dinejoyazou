Here Are the Best Flowers to Bring to Any Occasions

Flowers are traditional gifts to bestow at holidays, so it’s only right to go with a classic. Roses represent love of all kinds, making them the superior selection for a celebration. For Valentine’s Day and Mother’s Day to Thanksgiving and Christmas, roses are the best blooms for any occasion at any time of year. For additional significance, choose a color based on its meaning–red for romance, yellow for friendship, orange for happiness, pink for appreciation, and white for loyalty.

Bonus Blooms: No matter what the occasion, a cluster of morning glories is a colorful contender signifying affection. Alternatively, you could select from seasonal flora to salute a particular holiday.
