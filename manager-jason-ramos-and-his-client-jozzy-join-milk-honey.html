Manager Jason Ramos And His Client Jozzy Join Milk &amp; Honey

LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Los Angeles-based management firm Milk & Honey Music and Sports, announced the addition of manager Jason Ramos to the team along with the singing of the Grammy-winning songwriter and recording artist Jozzy.

Ramos joins Milk & Honey after stints in artist development at Roc Nation and after running his own artist management and brand marketing consultant company with a roster that included Jozzy, Lil Mexico Beats, and Paris Jordan.

“Joining Milk & Honey is incredibly exciting, and I am thrilled to be working with Lucas and Nic. This is a pivotal year for Jozzy, with the release of her upcoming album, her 2024 Grammy Awards, and songwriting success. I know that we are destined for a vast amount of success. Jason Ramos, Manager at Milk & Honey.

With three Grammys to her name, Jozzy broke through with her contribution to the “Old Town Road Remix” featuring Lil Nas X and Billy Ray Cyrus. Since then, she’s gone on to contribute to multiple platinum-selling records for artists such as Beyoncé, Summer Walker, 21 Savage, Metro Boomin, Don Toliver, Latto, Pharrell Williams, Madonna, and more.

“I’m excited to join the Milk & Honey team to see what we can all achieve together. 2024 is a big year for me and I feel like I’m in the right place to do it,” Jozzy stated.

“For the little magic left in our adult lives, and as insider as it may seem, the real magic is seeing what our clients do in the studio that makes them true experts at their craft. I continue to be in awe of Jozzy, who has that magic on a level I do not often see, as she joins a family of amazing songwriters, artists and storytellers. She will be a top global priority for the Milk & Honey team, and I couldn’t be more excited to have her join us.” stated Lucas Keller, founder of Milk & Honey.
