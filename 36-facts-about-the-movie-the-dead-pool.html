The Dead Pool is the fifth and final installment in the Dirty Harry film series.

Released in 1988, this action-packed crime thriller marked the end of Clint Eastwood’s iconic portrayal of the tough cop, Harry Callahan.

Known for his work as a stunt coordinator, Van Horn took the helm in bringing the thrilling world of The Dead Pool to life.
