Prince Harry fans swoon over young royal but 'missing' his 'iconic' era

Prince Harry once enjoyed the title of the Most Eligible Bachelor in the world and fans are revisiting one iconic look of the royal heartthrob that made him the dreamiest of all.

Now happily married to Suits star Meghan Markle, Prince Harry was once known as the wild royal whose bachelor day stories made regular headlines in tabloids. But the father of two showed he is a die-hard romantic at heart when he fell for Meghan.

Moreover, Harry‘s evolution is not just limited to his relationship, followers have also loved the transition of his fashion game. From clean saved days to a ginger beard, the 39-year-old royal knows how to rock any look and make it look regal.

Followers revisit Prince Harry’s iconic era

TikTok fans of the Duke of Sussex are bringing back Prince Harry’s iconic ‘no beard’ era. The followers claim everyone had a crush on teen Harry’s iconic chic fashion sense. A collection of Harry’s most ‘heartthrob’ looks from the no beard days on the social media platform are making fans swoon over him.

One excited fan wrote: “Forever missing his era without a beard.” Another fan echoed the sentiments sharing: “Teen Harry is so iconic.”

Prince Harry’s beard is a big part of his ‘identity’

The author of the explosive memoir Spare was once candid about his beard being a big part of his identity. The Duke of Sussex talked about his ‘argument’ with his brother Prince William right before his wedding where the latter suggested he should shave off his beard.

Harry revealed his initial thought about shaving his beard saying, he “would feel very, very different without a beard.” The royal told ITV’s Tom Bradby: “That’s hard for people to understand who’ve never grown a beard but hopefully those beard people out there will go, ‘yeah, I get that, I can understand’.”
