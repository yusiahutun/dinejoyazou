100 Funny WiFi Names to Consider in 2023: Top WiFi Names

There are two kinds of people in the world: Those who accept whatever Wi-Fi name their Internet company provides them with and those who spend precious time dreaming up the perfect funny Wi-Fi name to replace it with. If you fall into the latter camp, you'll be glad to know that we scoured the web for dozens of hilarious Wi-Fi names to get your creative juices flowing. Ahead, you'll find Wi-Fi names inspired by some of the most iconic films of our time, including Star Wars, The Princess Bride, and Harry Potter. You'll also uncover a bounty of funny Wi-Fi names that play on the lyrics of some of the most popular songs of the past 50 years, as well as names that pay homage to musical sensations like Bob Dylan and the Wu-Tang Clan. Not a pop culture lover? There are a variety of food-, knowledge-, and nostalgia-inspired Wi-Fi names to choose from, too. Point being, no matter your sense of humor, you're bound to find at least one funny Wi-Fi name that will have you itching to update your own. Happy browsing!
