FAQs

Q: What is the best time to visit Tehran?

A: The best time to visit Tehran is during spring (March to May) and autumn (September to November) when the weather is mild and pleasant.

Q: Is it safe to visit Tehran?

A: Tehran is generally a safe city to visit. However, it is always advisable to take normal precautions and stay aware of your surroundings.

Q: What are some must-visit attractions in Tehran?

A: Some must-visit attractions in Tehran include the Golestan Palace, National Museum of Iran, Milad Tower, Sa’dabad Complex, and the Grand Bazaar.

Q: What is the local cuisine like in Tehran?

A: Tehran offers a diverse range of delicious local cuisine, including kebabs, stews like Ghormeh Sabzi and Fesenjan, and delightful Persian sweets like Gaz and Sohan.

Q: How can I get around in Tehran?

A: Tehran has an extensive public transportation system, including a metro, buses, and taxis, which make it convenient to get around the city.

Q: Are there any day trips from Tehran?

A: Yes, there are several day trips you can take from Tehran, such as visiting the stunning Alborz Mountains, the charming village of Darband, or the historical city of Rey.
